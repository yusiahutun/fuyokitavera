Emma Watson stuns as she poses topless for boob-filled shoot

Treading the line of sexy and classy, the star can be seen on the cover of the iconic glossy in an innocent white gown.

However, inside the mag, Emma takes things to a whole new level posing in a skirt and not much more.

Clearly keen to sex-up her image, the star can be seen gazing into the camera as she leaves little to the imagination.

Relying on a shawl to protect her modesty, Emma oozes class as she flaunts 99% of her assets.

However, rather than fall into a trap like many celebs before her, Emma's big reveal is northing short of striking compared to many tacky exposés we have seen.

The snap comes just days after the former Harry Potter star was booed during a short press trip to London.

Emma had been booked for a busy day of promo for new Disney flick Beauty and the Beast.

However, despite chatting to press at Spencer House when it came to meet her fans in Leicester Square she was less than keen.

The star arrived at the back of the ionic cinema – despite hoards of supports gathering behind crash barriers at the front of the venue.

As she got out of the car a handful of savvy fans began to boo her with one on-looker shouting: "Too much of a diva to stop now are you," as she snuck off without so much as a wave.

A rep for Emma said at the time: "It was Disney's decision to use the rear entrance. All cast members used the rear entrance.

"Emma signed for fans and autograph hunters outside the after party at Spencer House."
