Who is Richard Henry Lee:

Richard Henry Lee is a famous Politician. He was born on January 20, 1732 and his birthplace is Virginia. Richard is also well known as, A president of the Continental Congress, a signer of the Articles of Confederation, and the author of the Second Continental Congress’ famous Lee Resolution, this colonial statesman was one of the major voices in the American colonies’ fight for independence from Great Britain.

Richard is originated from United States. He was a contemporary of American founding father and fellow Virginia statesman Patrick Henry.
