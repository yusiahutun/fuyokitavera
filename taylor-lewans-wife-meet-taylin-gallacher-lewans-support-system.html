Taylor Lewan's Wife: Meet Taylin Gallacher, Lewan's Support System

Taylin Gallacher and Taylor Lewan’s love story is truly inspiring. As Taylor Lewan’s wife, Gallacher has garnered considerable fame and adoration from NFL and romance enthusiasts alike. While the couple has yet to reveal how they met, Taylor’s social media post hints that they met ten days before Valentine’s Day 2016 in Arizona, igniting a whirlwind romance.

On February 14, 2023, he wished his “Extraordinary wife a happy Valentine’s Day.” Taylor noted that seven years ago, they sat together in Arizona on that very day, having met just ten days prior.

He added, “You were about to return to Nashville, and I was overcome with fear, uncertain if you’d ever come back. Yet, two weeks later, you returned to Arizona, and that’s when we got engaged. My love for you knows no bounds.”

The couple exchanged vows in Big Sur in July 2018, with their daughter gracing the occasion and another bundle of joy on the way.

Meet Taylin Gallacher’s Husband, Taylor

Taylor Curtis Lewan, born on July 22, 1991, is a prominent American football offensive tackle currently a free agent. Born in Sacramento, California, he is the son of Dave Lewan and Kelly Riley. His father, Dave, was also an offensive lineman at the University of Michigan.

Taylor began his football journey during high school, initially playing as a defensive end for Cactus Shadows in Cave Creek, Arizona. He transitioned to Chaparral High School, where he excelled as an offensive lineman.

Taylor’s talent and athleticism garnered recognition during his high school years. He was regarded as a four-star prospect, ranking fifth among Arizona players and 194th nationally. He was also recognized as the third-most athletic offensive lineman and the fifth-most agile offensive lineman. His remarkable abilities led to his selection for the prestigious Under Armour All-America Game.

Continuing his football career at the University of Michigan, Taylor succeeded wildly. He received first-team All-American honors twice and established himself as one of the top offensive linemen in college football. In 2014, he was chosen by the Tennessee Titans in the first round of the NFL Draft, launching his professional career.

He also earned three consecutive Pro Bowl selections from 2016 to 2018, showcasing his dominance on the field. Additionally, he was recognized as part of the PFWA All-Rookie Team and received the Big Ten Offensive Lineman of the Year award.

Taylor Lewan’s Wife Is His Greatest Support System

During an interview with Tennessean in 2017, Taylor shared how much his wife has changed his life. When he met Gallacher, he was in a state of confusion. He wasn’t satisfied with the direction of his career and life. However, she inspired some transformation in him.

Taylor revealed that meeting his wife helped set him up in his career and changed his outlook on life. He said he won’t have been an integral part of his team had he not met Gallacher. In his words, his wife and kids are the best things that have happened to him.

Taylin Gallacher Gushes Over Her Husband

Similarly, the love Gallacher has for Taylor is so heartwarming. She often shares pictures of herself and her spouse on her Instagram handle, confessing her love for him. After their wedding in 2018, Gallacher expressed her deep affection for Taylor on Instagram. Her heartfelt message recounts their anguish after his severe concussion in 2017 and their subsequent struggle to overcome it.

However, the 2018 concussion wasn’t Taylor’s only health challenge. In October 2022, he underwent surgery, and Gallacher shared a sweet photograph of herself, Taylor, and their two little daughters, Wynne Rebel Lewan and Willow Lewan.

She wrote:

“As I anxiously await my beloved’s recovery from surgery, I am reminded of the mountain we’ve already conquered together. I am eternally grateful to all those who have reached out during these past few weeks, even if I haven’t had a chance to respond.”

Gallacher once confessed that she could hardly believe Taylor was real. She expressed her love and appreciation for his kindness and unwavering support. On his birthday in 2017, shortly after the birth of their eldest daughter, Wynne, she penned a touching message:

“Happy birthday to the epitome of perfection. You are a gift beyond measure. Thank you for our daughter, the endless laughter, and the joy that surpasses imagination. It’s hard for me to believe that you’re real. I love you.”

She also shared endearing snapshots of Taylor tenderly cuddling baby Wynne and engaging in activities like reading bedtime stories, visiting the aquarium, and enjoying merry-go-round rides with their daughters.

Taylor Lewan Was Involved in a Cheating Rumor

Gallacher and Taylor’s marriage might seem like a match made in heaven, but it has been subject to some rumors. Taylor was linked to YouTube sensation and make-up artist Jeffree Star. Fans were taken aback when the rumors started going around.

The rumors started when the cosmetics mogul hinted at having an NFL boyfriend on social media. On February 10, 2023, he revealed that his mysterious “NFL boo” was Taylor Lewan.

Star added fuel to the fire by sharing a TikTok video of Taylor with his back turned and captioned it, “His team didn’t make it to the Super Bowl… but he’s 6’6″ and plays perfect in the bedroom.” He even posted pictures of their hands intertwined, claiming they were on a flight from LA to Wyoming.

However, it was soon revealed that Taylor and Star were creating excitement for Star’s appearance on Taylor’s “Bussin With the Boys” podcast. Fans were glad Taylor was not cheating on Gallacher, and their love remained untainted.

Rumored love affairs involving celebrities are not uncommon. Chris Meloni once found himself in a rumor about a love affair with a co-star.
