Marlon Wayans dismisses claims he 'accidentally' posted something naked on Instagram

Marlon Wayans is no stranger to nudity in his movies, which he poked fun at in a new Instagram post for April Fools Day.

The actor had seemingly posted an Instagram Story video that made it look like he was naked, when he actually wasn't, so he decided to turn the tables on his fans.

On April Fool's Day, he posted proof that he wasn't naked in the video, along with shots of him actually nude, from his 2017 Netflix movie Naked and his 1997 comedy Senseless.
