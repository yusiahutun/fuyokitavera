Blackpink x Touchland Power Mist Launch 2023

There are plenty of reasons to carry hand sanitizer around, but viral K-pop group Blackpink just gave us another.

The singing sensations have collaborated with personal-care brand Touchland for a signature Blackpink take on the highly popular Power Mist Hydrating Hand Sanitizer. The limited-edition launch features a pink text and border design, along with a see-through black glass center, and Touchland’s vegan, 70 percent ethyl alcohol formula. And to minimize any lingering alcohol smell as you spritz, it also includes the brand’s best-selling Blue Sandalwood scent, comprised of key fragrance notes like lemon, velvet peach, and gold amber.

Touchland

Touchland

As Touchland is a woman-owned company beloved of stars like Kim Kardashian and noncelebrity beauty enthusiasts alike, partnering with Blackpink on this release is especially meaningful to its founder. “As a woman-owned brand, Blackpink symbolizes female empowerment and continues to show how confident women can be,” CEO Andrea Lisbona, who started the company, said in a statement. “I hope our community and their fanbase, Blinks, enjoy this partnership as much as I do.”

Even if you missed Blackpink’s 18-song performance at this year’s Coachella festival, you can still snag this exclusive piece of the band’s coveted merch—while it’s still in stock.

The Blackpink x Touchland Power Mist Hydrating Hand Sanitizer retails for $10 and is available to shop now at touchland.com.
