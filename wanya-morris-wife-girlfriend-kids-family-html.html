Wanya Morris and His Wife's Love Story!

MusicBy Rushali Kapali | On: July 11, 2022

Boyz II Men was one of the sensational R&B bands of the 1990s. One of its members, Wanya Morris, is considered to be the luckiest one to get married to his best friend. 

So who is Wanya Morris' wife? What do we know about the singer's love story? 

Follow through to learn about his married life and if he has children.

Wanya Morris' Wife

The End Of The Road singer married his girlfriend, Amber Reyes, in 2019. Wanya's wife, Amber, is the founder of a boutique spa, LA Laser. 

He hasn't provided any information on how he met his wife, but their Instagram posts indicate they have known one other since 2016. So maybe their love ignited after they met on the 'Dancing with the Stars' set.

The couple had a private wedding with their close friends and family in The Ritz Carlton, Netherlands. 

The decorations for the couple's nuptials were white according to their theme. The groom wore an off-white suit with a white shirt, while the bride wore a white deep-neck wedding gown with a long veil.

Wanya Morris's Kids

It was big news to all the singer's fans when Wanya and Amber's wedding news surfaced in 2019 because

However, Wanya and his ex-partner were seen together in 2018 and never shared anything regarding their divorce.

Before marrying Amber, Wanya was married to an American designer Traci Nash. The couple got married in 2002, but there are no details about their divorce. 

Wanya and his former wife, Traci, share six kids, four sons, and two daughters. The couple has not disclosed their daughters' names; however, their sons' names are quite unique. 

Wanya's kids with their mother. (Photo: Instagram)

The oldest of them is Wanya II, popularly known as Big Boy, followed by Wanya III, known as Chulo; Wanya IV, known as Tyvas; and the youngest, Wanya V, known as Rocco.

Wanya's children have followed in his footsteps. His four sons have formed a group named, WanMor: Brotherly Harmony, which is a tribute to Wanya. 

The boys had performed on international platforms like 'America's Got Talent' and Nickelodeon's show, 'America's Most Musical Family.' 

Besides his marriage, Wanya was also in a relationship with his fellow R&B singer, Brandy Norwood. In the 1990s, Wanya's relationship with his ex-girlfriend was well known.

The couple started as a friend, and slowly they fell in love. Although they are not together anymore, they still share a wonderful friendship. Wanya claims to have helped Brandy make her show, 'Moesha,' a smashing success.

Wanya, age 48, prefers to keep his personal life away from the public, but his career is well known to the public.
