REVEALED: The four-ingredient slow cooker Christmas cake recipe that will 'change your life'

A home cook has revealed the best and easiest Christmas cake recipe - and all you need is a slow cooker and four ingredients.

The cake's recipe is easy to reproduce, but requires time to let the fruit soak properly before it can be cooked.

All that is needed for this recipe is flour, a kilo of dried fruit, chocolate milk and some Bailey's Irish Cream.

The milk and Bailey's are poured over the fruit which is then left to soak for 24 hours.

After that the flour is added and the mixture poured into the slow cooker for six to eight hours. 

The recipe was shared on the slow cooker recipe and tips Facebook page, after budding home cooks asked for a simple Christmas cake.

WHAT'S THE RECIPE FOR SLOW COOKER CHRISTMAS CAKE? 

INGREDIENTS

1 kg mixed fruit (You can also add a packet of glacé cherries)

2 Cups Chocolate Milk  

1/2-3/4 Cup Baileys

2 Cups self raising Flour

METHOD 

 Soak the fruit in  the chocolate milk and  Baileys for 24 hours.

Fold in the flour. Line slow cooker with two layers of baking paper on bottom and sides. 

You can also double a long strip of baking paper and put in first so easier to pull out when cooked. 

 Pour batter into the slow cooker and even it out. 

Cook on low for 7-8 hours, it could be ready in 6 if you have a hot slow cooker. 

Make sure you cook with a tea towel under lid. 

NOTE

The tea towel is to collect condensation that forms on the lid, keeping it from falling onto the cake, keeping the top of the cake dry.

The original recipe used only three ingredients, the home cook added the Bailey's for more flavor .

The packet of glacé cherries are an additional option.
