Vicki Lawrence Biography TimeLine

Vicki Lawrence is an American actress, comedian, singer, and television personality. She is best known for her role as Thelma Harper, or “Mama,” on the hit TV series “Mama’s Family,” as well as her appearances on “The Carol Burnett Show.” Here is a timeline of her life and career:

  • March 26th 1949: Vicki Lawrence was born in Inglewood, California, United States.
  • 1967: At 18, Lawrence auditioned for “The Carol Burnett Show” and was hired as a regular cast member.
  • 1972: She recorded the hit song “The Night the Lights Went Out in Georgia,” which reached No. 1 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart.
  • 1983: “Mama’s Family” premiered on NBC, with Lawrence reprising her role as Thelma Harper, a character she had played on “The Carol Burnett Show.”
  • 1990-2000: Lawrence continued to act in TV shows and films and appeared on various talk shows and game shows.
  • 2012: She published a memoir, “Vicki!: The True Life Adventures of Miss Fireball,” which detailed her career and personal life.
  • 2019: Lawrence reprised her role as Mama for a live stage tour, “Vicki Lawrence and Mama: A Two Woman Show,” featuring a Q&A with the audience.

Throughout her career, Vicki Lawrence has proven to be a versatile and talented performer with acting, comedy, singing, and hosting skills. Her portrayal of Mama in “Mama’s Family” has become iconic. Her contributions to “The Carol Burnett Show” helped make it one of the most incredible comedy series ever. Lawrence’s talents have earned her numerous awards and nominations, and her enduring popularity is a testament to her enduring talent.

Personal Life of Vicki Lawrence

Vicki Lawrence has been in marital relationships twice throughout her lifetime. She was primarily married to American singer and songwriter Bobby Russell. The wedding ceremony was held on June 29, 1972. Unfortunately, the couple split way back in 1974 and ended the relationship with a divorce.

Shortly after that, Vicki started dating Al Schultz, a make-up artist whom she met during the set of The Carol Burnett Show in 1967. The couple shared a close relationship and eventually decided to tie the knot on November 16, 1974.

The American musician married Al Schultz and are parents to two lovely children, Garrett Lawrence Schultz and Courtney Allison Schultz. In 2011, Vicki was diagnosed with chronic idiopathic urticaria (CIU), a chronic hives with undistinguished causes. The singer has shared her story and shed light on the CIU & You campaign.
