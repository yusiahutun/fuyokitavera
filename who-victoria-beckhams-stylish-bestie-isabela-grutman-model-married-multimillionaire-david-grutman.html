Who is Victoria Beckhams stylish bestie Isabela Grutman? The model is married to multimillionaire

The Miami-based “It” girl was spotted sitting front row this year at Paris Fashion Week shows, where she watched Victoria Beckham and Acne Studios runways; has accumulated nearly half a million followers on Instagram; and designed a new fashion collection called “Le Sud” – an ode to the savoir faire of Southern France, inviting A-list pals like Lionel Messi’s wife Antonela Roccuzzo, influencer Alix Earle and, of course, VB to the showcase presented at Casadonna restaurant this week.

So, where did she come from, and who is she exactly? Here’s everything you need to know.

Isabela Grutman hangs out with the Beckhams, Kardashian-Jenners and Messis

The 31-year-old influencer, who hails from Belo Horizonte, Brazil, has not only become a luxury fashion fixture, but also a part of society’s “It” girl scene.

She hangs out with David and Victoria Beckham (and their kids), Lionel Messi and his wife Antonela Roccuzzo, Kendall Jenner, Kim Kardashian, Peggy Gou, Dua Lipa, Donatella Versace, Eva Longoria, Hailey Bieber and Camila Cabello.

Although it may seem like she’s one of VB’s newer pals, their friendship actually dates back longer.

In 2021, Grutman even gave her friend a shoutout in an interview with L’Officiel India, stating, “At the moment I love Victoria Beckham serum and priming moisturiser,” when asked about her beauty routine.

She runs a fashion and jewellery brand

Grutman has her hands in both fashion and jewellery companies.

According to her LinkedIn, she is the CEO of Rangel, a luxury resortwear brand founded in 2021, and Isa Grutman, which offers elegant jewellery pieces and was started in 2022.

Her profile also states that she works as a concept designer for Groot Hospitality, is a partner at IGK Hair, and is the vice-president of Style Saves – a non-profit that provides students with clothing, school supplies and more.

She’s married to a hospitality multimillionaire

Isabela married Miami-based hospitality multimillionaire David Grutman in 2016. The 49-year-old entrepreneur is behind dining concepts like Gekko, Komodo, Swan, The Key Club, Papi Steak and Strawberry Moon.

In an interview with the Michelin Guide, David shared insights into his relationship, saying that the key to a successful marriage is to have “deep love, patience and support”. He added, “Isabela is beyond supportive – we find ways to keep our family life separate from work, but we also fuse them when it makes sense.”

She’s a model for Wilhelmina

Grutman is signed with Wilhelmina Models, one of the biggest agencies in the biz. But she’s been modelling for most of her life and started when she was just 13, she told L’Officiel India: “I looked much older, so I remember my first job being a catalogue for gowns and it was the first time I tried one.”

She has modelled for Adidas, Polo and L’Oréal over the years.

She has two daughters

Grutman lives a busy life as a doting mama to two girls, whom she shares with her hubby. Their most recent daughter was born in 2019. Celebrity friends like Kim K, Vanessa Hudgens, Nick Jonas, Pharrell Williams, Sofia Richie, Winnie Harlow, Gabrielle Union and Scooter Braun all commented on the baby’s birth post to express their congratulations to the now mum-of-two.
