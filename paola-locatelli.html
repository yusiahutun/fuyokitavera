Paola Locatelli Bio, Age, Height, Boyfriend, Net Worth

Paola Locatelli is a known French personality who has developed into an influencer, model, actress, and entrepreneur. She started earning a name for herself in 2015 when she was only 11 years old and started by publishing videos on the YouTube platform.

Early Life

Paola Locatelli was born on 2nd March 2004 in Thionville, Moselle, to her loving and caring parents, Aline Lopes (mother) and Patrice Locatelli (father). Her family moved to Paris, taking all children with them (when Paola was only 2), and since then, the whole family has been raised there. She shared her childhood with her one brother Gabriel and a sister named Mélissa. The sister is also available on her YouTube channel and is often seen coordinating her thoughts and acts.

She had the first social media account on her name on Twitter, registering herself in 2014.

After that, the advertising campaign, #VeetParlonsPoils, called on her in 2019.
