Camille Chen's biography

The actress was born on 1st September 1979 in Taiwan. Her father is Taiwanese, while her mother is American. Camille and her mother relocated to the United States after her parents separated.

Camille's mother managed to give her a good upbringing despite being a single mother living in the US. The State Farm Spy commercial actress shared a close bond with her mother.

She does not shy away from sharing lovely pictures with adorable captions to celebrate her. For instance, during 2016's Father Day, she shared a major throwback picture of her mother captioning that she is two in one. This referred to her mother being the father, business owner, entrepreneur, friend, and general badass.

Chen was raised in the United States, where she studied for her elementary and high school studies. After high school graduation, she enrolled at the University of Texas in Austin. She graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Theater.

Career

Chen began her career immediately after completing university studies. She started doing voiceover for the English version of two anime television series, Lost Universe and Devil Lady. Her first movie features were in two uncredited roles.

She appeared as Miss Hampshire in Miss Congeniality in 2000 and as a cheerleader in The New Guy in 2002. Between 2006 and 2007, she starred as Samantha Li in Studio 60 on the Sunset Strip. In 2009, she appeared as a guest star in Just a Girl in the World episode of Law and Order.
