The ravishing meteorologist, Ivan Cabrera is also an actor for [[Yingo]] (2007), [[Don Diablo]] (2010) and [[Gordis]] (2012) and Chilean dancer. He is the weather anchor or weather forecaster on CNN international. Currently, he works for ABC action news as a primary meteorologist during the weekend with the team named, “the most accurate in the state of Florida.” Ivan has not revealed much information about his personal life.

Early Life and Education

Ivan, the award-winning broadcasting meteorologist, was born on 11th February 1983 in Cuba. Manual Ivan Cabrera Salinas was his birth name, and EL Potro is his nickname.  Later on, he was brought up in Miami.  Ivan is Florida State University graduate and holds AMS Television Seal of Approval from the American Meteorological Society. Cabrera has a keen interest in dancing right from his childhood.  He used to participate in every school dancing events. Michael Jackson is a source of inspiration for Ivan.

Career

Cabrera's career began from AP network news and local radio station across the United States where he worked as a radio broadcaster. Ivan latterly joined CNN as a primary meteorologist. In 2014, he left CNN and was appointed to ABC action news as a meteorologist. He also served for WSI Corporation for the same job which included a regular appearance on FOX Channel.

Talking about his achievements, he was awarded by American Meteorological Society Television Seal of Approval Emmy- Team Tornado Coverage- WESH-TV-2007 and Peabody Award- Gulf Oil Spill Coverage –CNN-2010.

Personal Life

Ivan Cabrera got married to Tiffany Magrini on 6th September 2014, and they have one child. However, he has not shared any information about his child on social media sites. Maybe the couple wants to give their child a normal life away from the cameras.
