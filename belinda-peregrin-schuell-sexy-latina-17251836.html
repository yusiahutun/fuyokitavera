Stunning footage of sexy Latina singer LEVITATED by magician goes viral

Belinda Peregrin Schuell, known to her fans as Belinda, posted footage of the baffling illusion on social media to share with her followers.

The 27-year-old singer was delighted when magician Criss Angel, 48, invited her to take part in one of his shows.

In the video the singer, who was in the headlines recently after showing off her skills at breaking an apple with her head, is lying in a swimming pool for the stunt and is levitated into the air by the magician.

In order to do the trick, he seems to put her into some sort of trance.

The footage has been seen over 330,000 times since it was shared online.

Magician Criss also shared the video on his Instagram account with the message "look at how I scared the Latin star Belinda is in my new special #TRICKD that will be broadcast in October."

He also wrote: "This is Belinda, she is one of the most popular Latin pop stars. She has just been in my show, and I am wondering if at some stage she might be able to do the apple trick for me."

In June the Latin pop sensation shocked fans when she repeatedly tried and eventually succeeded to smash an apple using her head on a morning show in Mexico.

It then turned out she had often performed the same stunt, starting in 2011 when she participated in another show. She took an apple and smashed it several times against her head to break it in two. After she did it, she said:

"I have a very hard head."

It is as of yet unclear why the singer does this as the only thing she mentioned is that it proves she has a hard head.
