Meghan Markle wore Princess Diana's ring to the wedding reception

Meghan Markle made a tribute to her late mother-in-law, Diana, Princess of Wales, at the royal wedding reception by wearing her Asprey Aquamarine ring.

The new Duchess of Sussex was seen wearing the emerald cut aquamarine as she left Windsor Castle to Frogmore House where the reception was held. The sparkling ring, surrounded by small solitaire diamonds, is set in 24 carat yellow gold and was created by Asprey in 1997.

The blue piece made even more impact against the her sleek white gown by Stella McCartney. Diana, Princess of Wales was photographed wearing the ring at a Christie's auction of her dresses in London in 1997.

The late royal left the majority of her jewellery to her sons so that they could one day give it to their respective wives. She wrote in her will, "I would like you to allocate all my jewellery to the share to be held by my sons, so that their wives may, in due course, have it or use it."

Prince Harry has said before that he believes that had his late mother met Markle the two would have been "thick as thieves".
