More proof Travis Kelce's true gentleman nature with Taylor Swift isn't a gimmick

The Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce content just keeps on coming, and he’s once again proving he’s a gentleman on their latest date night, to an Argentine Steakhouse restaurant.

The celebrity couple were spotted out together earlier this week, and once again, Travis Kelce was seen being the protective gentleman Swifties have all seen and loved.

Travis Kelce’s ‘gentlemanly’ actions towards Taylor Swift

The two were seen rolling up to the swanky restaurant in a Rolls Royce, in pictures obtained by the Daily Mail.

It’s said that the hottest new celebrity couple was dining in the establishment for two hours in Kansas City, even having the whole restaurant to themselves at one point, as they stayed open late to accommodate.

After their slap-up meal, the pair were seen walking out holding hands, to the car before the NFL star placed his hands on Taylor Swift‘s lower back, before opening the car door, making sure the music star was safely inside.

It’s not the first time the 34-year-old has been seen helping Swift into the car. A few weeks ago, he sent Swifites wild as he ushered a bodyguard out of the way, before opening the door for her himself.

As the clip went viral on X, formerly known as Twitter, Jason Kelce asked his brother on their podcast, New Heights, if he feels like he needs to act as the Love Story star’s ‘bodyguard.’

In response, he said: “I feel like whenever I’m on a date I’m always having the sense, like, I’m a man in the situation. I’m protective.”

After fans got over the fangirling of that video, it started all over again as the duo were spotted sharing a kiss in public for the very first time. Honestly, it’s all too much!

Taylor has the approval of the Kelce family

Although it’s not been long, it looks like Taylor already has the approval of Travis’ family. She’s been seen sitting with his parents at Chief games, and his dad has now spoken out about how “sweet” the star is.

In an interview with PEOPLE, Ed Kelce gushed about how she was “a very, very sweet, very charming, down-to-earth young woman.”

He also went on to tell the publication about “something special” he noticed on the first meeting.

“We’re sitting in the suite, she gets up and in the front room, she gets up to go get a drink or something and she starts picking up empty bottles, cans, plates that are scattered around. Because in the suites everybody gets stuff and you empty it down wherever you can.”

It’s not only Ed who appears to be a fan of the relationship. Earlier this week, brother Jason told NBC “I’m happy for my brother that he seems to be in a relationship that he’s excited about, that he is genuine about.”

However, he did raise some ‘alarm bells’ with the amount of press attention his brother was now getting.
