Lily James Celebrates Her 35th Birthday in a Polka-Dot Minidress

Glitter and sequins might be the go-to when it comes to birthday outfits, but Lily James proved you don’t always need shimmer to shine on your special day.

The Iron Claw star celebrated turning the big 3-5 last night with a fete at private club Apollo’s Muse in London. For the occasion, she looked oh so chic in a neutral-colored ensemble with fun touches throughout. James came party-ready in an asymmetrical minidress that featured a one-shoulder neckline and draping details throughout, all in gauzy cloudy-white fabric with a muted green polka-dot pattern emblazoned across it. She kept warm with a long khaki-brown trench coat and added reflective-gold peep-toe sandals with a short heel and, for a touch of bling, a jewel-adorned ankle strap. For beauty, James went with soft glam, complete with a berry lip and a glossy red manicure, and styled her new bronde locks in soft bouncy waves. She finished everything off with diamond-adorned stringy drop earrings and a dainty chain necklace.

Neil P. Mockford//Getty Images

The English actor often opts for sophisticated ensembles with a bit of quirky flair. In February, while arriving at the Prada fashion show during Milan Fashion Week, she traded her neutrals for a darker palette. Then, she captivated in a black high-neck dress that featured a stringy skirt and a whimsical sequin swirl pattern, paired with a boxy deep ocean-blue denim jacket, inky-black satin Mary Jane sandals with a dramatic curved pointed-toe detail, a small black purse, and matching shades.

Victor Boyko//Getty Images

Earlier in the month, she walked the red carpet for the premiere of her new film Finalmente l’Alba looking like an ethereal vision in a sweeping white halterneck dress by Valentino. The centerpiece featured tiers of gauzy layers, white feather embellishments, and a flirty open back.
