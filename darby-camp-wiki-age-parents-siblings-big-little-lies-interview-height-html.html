Darby’s Introduction to Acting:

It's not just the little girl that’s interested in acting; her mother is also active in the entertainment industry. Even before being introduced to performing, she indulged into the world of imagination and acted. It made her mom look out for the role she could play in. 

She got her first job at the age of six playing in Drop Dead Diva followed by the TV movie The Summers Sisters. Her big break came with the show she is best known for, i.e., Big Little Lies where she plays Chloe Mackenzie.

She continues to work on The Leftovers as a recurring character and recently appeared on Grey’s Anatomy. She is also set to appear on the series Benji.
