Cartel Investigation | DMZ Missions Guide in Warzone

Cartel Investigation is a new Season 3 DMZ Mission for the faction Redacted featured in the DMZ mode of Call of Duty: Warzone 2.

In this guide, you can find all the mission's objectives and the rewards you'll obtain by completing it.

Cartel Investigation Mission Info

  • Tier 1
  • Release Season 3
  • Faction Redacted
  • Rewards Double XP Token
    +5000 XP

Cartel Investigation Mission Tasks:

  • Locate the cartel in Al Mazrah
  • Kill 5 cartel soldiers
  • Find and extract 2 large Contraband Packages
