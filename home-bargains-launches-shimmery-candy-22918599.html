Home Bargains launches shimmery candy cane and show fairy gin for Christmas

The festive season is approaching…

We may not have celebrated Halloween yet, but that hasn’t stopped a flood of Christmas products hitting shelves.

And, the latest offering from the Manchester Drinks Company is sure to get you feeling jolly.

The new candy cane gin liqueur is a small-batch booze option which we can’t wait to try.

The beverage combines a subtle mint flavour with sweet vanilla to create a nostalgic sweetshop taste.

Try mixing it with ice and tonic water or even a flavoured hard seltzer – we like Two Brooks – for a tasty hi-ball drink.

You can even give it a stir with a candy cane garnish!

The glittery, pinky red gin will impress pals at dinner parties.

Plus, it’s a brilliant budget option at just £8 for 500ml.

Richard Benjamin, Director at Manchester Drinks said: “Christmas is fast approaching and what better way to get in the mood than with festive gin!

“We know that our fans love our nostalgic flavours so we wanted to create something we knew they’d love with added festive sparkle.

“This fresh and festive gin is perfect to enjoy on cosy nights in or with friends and family over the Christmas period.”

The 20% ABV liqueur will be available at Home Bargains from November 6.

Plus, Manchester Drinks Company’s popular snow fairy gin is back – the shimmery tipple tastes like cinnamon, spice and orange and also costs £8 at Home Bargains.
