Inside Olivia Rodrigo's Guts Tour! The 21-year-old pop star FLOATS over the crowd on a crescent moon

Olivia Rodrigo kicked off her 77-date Guts World Tour at the Acrisure Arena in Thousand Palms, CA on Friday night while perched atop a crescent moon.

The celestial moment happened as the Cali-born pop star - who turned 21 on Tuesday - sang Logical and Enough for You while floating through the 11,000-capacity venue.

'I'm so happy to be in Palm Springs. I've never played here before. But I grew up in Temecula, which is not too far,' Olivia told the audience.

'So um there's sort of a Riverside karmic system. Did anyone go to my last tour, my Sour Tour? That's so cool. Well it's nice to see you guys again. Y'all look prettier than I remember. Is it anybody's first concert in the house? Really, that's so cool. Thanks for spending it with me.'

Rodrigo wore the same black bra-top and high-waisted shorts beneath a fishnet mini-dress to perform her track Happier while seated onstage with a guitarist.

One major difference between the Sour Tour and this tour in support of her second studio album was Olivia hired eight back-up dancers to perform alongside her.

Rodrigo performed her tracks Love is Embarrassing and Pretty Isn't Pretty with the fleet of professional hoofers.

The half-Filipino beauty began her concert wearing a silver sparkly bra-top and matching mini-skirt selected by styling duo Chloe & Chenelle Delgadillo.

Hairstylist Clayton Hawkins gave a bit of wave to Olivia's signature waist-length natural mane and make-up artist Melissa Hernandez applied her bedazzled eyes and bright red pout.

Rodrigo wore the eye-catching costume to perform her tracks Vampire, Traitor, and Bad Idea, Right?

The self-described 'Spicy Pisces' later showcased her prowess on the piano while belting her tracks Teenage Dream and Drivers License.

Olivia also got to show off her skills on the electric guitar during her 95-minute inaugural show.

Rodrigo - who's dating Argylle actor Louis Partridge - donned a red-sequin halter romper to perform Brutal and bonus track Obsessed. 

According to Rolling Stone, the former Disney Channel star replaced the sanitized line 'perfect all-American hips' to 'perfect all-American t**s' while running her hands over her breasts during All-American Bi***.

For the encore, Olivia emerged clutching a bright red megaphone while clad in a white 'Just a Girl' tank top as a tribute to No Doubt frontwoman Gwen Stefani.

Rodrigo's finals songs of the night were Get Him Back and Good 4 U as star-shaped confetti rained down on the roaring crowd.

The White House's Covid vaxx advocate then climbed down so she could physically embrace her fans, who went wild as she greeted them for several minutes.

Olivia will be donating a portion of all her ticket sales from the Guts World Tour to Fund 4 Good - her new global initiative for women, girls, and people seeking reproductive health freedom.
