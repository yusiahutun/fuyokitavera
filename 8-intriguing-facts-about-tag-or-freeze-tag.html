8 Intriguing Facts About Tag (or Freeze Tag)

Source: Teachingexpertise.com

Tag, also known as freeze tag, is a classic childhood game that has been enjoyed by generations of children. It’s a simple game with one person designated as “it” who must chase and touch the other players in order to “freeze” them. While tag may seem like a simple game on the surface, there are actually some fascinating facts and tidbits about this beloved pastime. From its ancient origins to its inclusion in professional sports, tag has a rich and intriguing history. In this article, we will explore eight captivating facts about tag, shedding light on the game’s cultural significance and its enduring popularity. So, get ready to reminisce about your childhood and discover some interesting facts about tag that you may not have known before!
