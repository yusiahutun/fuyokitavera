Kim Zolciak-Biermann Says 'RHOA' Co-Stars Phaedra Parks Is 'Sneaky' and Kenya Moore Is a 'Lunatic' (

After the explosive conclusion of of "The Real Housewives of Atlanta" reunion, Kim Zolciak-Biermann stopped by "Watch What Happens Live" to dish about her feelings towards Phaedra Parks, feud with Kenya Moore and her son Kash's recovery since a dog attack.

Zolciak-Biermann is set to return part-time to the series next season and told host Andy Cohen that her relationship with Kandi Burruss has greatly improved.

"I'm irritated that she never cleared up the fact that I didn't steal Kash's name and that she was OK on 'Don't Be Tardy' and paid well, you know?" she said. "So I just wanted to stress that to her, and she said, 'We're good. We're good.' And I moved on."

On Phaedra Parks' "RHOA" accusation that Kandi Burruss had schemed to drug Porsha Williams and take her home:

"I think she's sneaky and I think she's gotta be on something to just have no expression and no emotion. She's just like not there, I felt like."

"Kandi's a Taurus - She's May 17th, I'm May 19th, so we're pretty close. I get her. It's been five years. We had a pretty good friendship. So I just wanted to stress that to her, and she said, 'We're good. We're good.' And I moved on. Kandi is not a bad person. What you see is what you get pretty much with her."

"I know she's been with women in the past and that sort of thing, and I've also hear about her in her marriage with somebody else, which would never happen in mine, but I don't know her and I'm not close to her to even answer that. But I do know Tauruses like sex."
