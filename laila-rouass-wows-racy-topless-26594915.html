Laila Rouass wows in racy topless snap after marriage split

Laila Rouass has sent fans wild with a sultry selfie as she posed for her Instagram followers.

The 50-year-old actress, best known for her roles in Footballers’ Wives and Holby City, was married to snooker player Ronnie O’Sullivan for 10 years before their recent split.

They went their separate ways in February and she has since taken to Instagram to show off her single life.

Laila posted a picture of herself relaxing in a bedroom for her 32,000 followers, much to their delight.

The 50-year-old wore her long dark hair in loose curls and complimented the look with smokey eyeshadow and nude lipstick.

She pouted in front of a backdrop of green striped wallpaper and a bed covered in white sheets, brightly lit by a lamp.

The actress, who came fourth in Strictly Come Dancing in 2009, appeared to be topless as she held the camera up for the selfie.

She simply captioned the picture with: “Tuesday night.”

Rouass has been in a number of iconic British television series, most notably for her portrayal of Amber Gates in ITV’s Footballers’ Wives.

She has also had recurring roles in Family Affairs and Hollyoaks, with appearances in Casualty, Primeval, Spooks and The Sarah Jane Adventures.

Rouass also competed in the seventh series of BBC’s dance competition Strictly Come Dancing alongside her fellow Footballers’ Wives star Zoe Lucker.

She married a family friend in 1990, with whom she divorced in 2003, and went on to live with her new partner, with whom she shares a daughter.

The father of her child was sentenced to nine years in prison in 2011 for participating in a £250 million VAT fraud conspiracy.

She began dating snooker star Ronnie O’Sullivan in 2012 and the pair were engaged just a year later, remaining together until just last month.

Laila shared the sad news on her Instagram after a decade of engagement with O’Sullivan, who is widely regarded as one of the most talented players in the history of snooker.
