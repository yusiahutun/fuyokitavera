He plays the monstrous title role in his new film I, Frankenstein, which disappointed at the US box office.

But Aaron Eckhart looked every inch the hot-blooded man on Thursday.

The 45-year-old showed off his impressive physique and fitness skills during a beachside photo shoot in Santa Monica.

Looking good: Aaron Eckhart showed off his impressive physique as he worked out during a beachside photo shoot in Santa Monica, CA on Thursday

The star was seen working out shirtless for the camera, displaying his strength as he pumped weights and did push-ups.

Aaron wore wet black shorts and grey trainers with neon green shoelaces.

He was seen jogging along the beach, his hair wet from the rain.

Fighting fit: Aaron wore wet black shorts and grey trainers with neon green shoelaces as he jogged in the rain

Now you're just showing off! Aaron also did some push-ups, displaying his strength

The Thank You For Smoking actor also adjusted his shorts while a stylist touched up his hair.

Later he stood in the cold surf, running through the waves as the camera caught him in action.

Aaron played a hands-on role in production, checking out the stills from the day on the director's computer.

Braving the cold: Aaron also stood in the surf, running through the waves as the camera caught him in action

The handsome star seemed in good spirits despite the box office disappointment of his new film.

Aaron plays infamous monster Frankenstein who, though created in Mary Shelley's 1795 novel, finds himself in modern times battling two supernatural forces.

The film only brought in $8.3 million over its opening weekend, a dismal start for a movie that cost $65 million to make and millions to promote.

Star power: The Thank You For Smoking actor also adjusted his shorts while a stylist touched up his hair

'It's a contemporary story about the monster, the unwanted so-called abortion, the aberration, the unworthy son created and thrown out of the house,' Aaron told Yahoo! Movies in a recent interview.

'And he has to go find his soul, find his purpose in life, and he does that in a fantastical world with gargoyles and demons. While these demons are against him and the gargoyles are sort of for him in a way.'

The movie also co-stars Jai Courtney, Bill Nighy, Yvonne Strahovski and Miranda Otto.
