Elsa Hosk Shows Her Love for Indie Designers

Dress, Cecilie Bahnsen, mytheresa.com.

BAZAAR.com asked model and influencer Elsa Hosk to self-style her favorite independently owned fashion brands including Tove, Christopher Esber, Victor Glemaud, Cecilie Bahnsen, and more, as a way to spotlight small businesses now.

Hosk has a vested interest in giving some love to the proverbial little guy. "My boyfriend started a brand a few years ago and a lot of my friends run smaller brands, so I see firsthand how tough it is to stay afloat during a pandemic or a recession. I know how hard they work and how much they appreciate the support. If you know you can put your money towards someone that needs it to stay in business right now, I would 100 percent do that," she says.

The Swedish beauty's effortless approach to style and how she showcases it on her Instagram account (with a casual 6.2 million followers) is doubtlessly influential. "I think a piece of clothing itself inspires me to put together an outfit a certain way," she says. "I like to look and feel like myself and dress to lift whatever mood I'm in. Inspiration can come from anywhere—movies, pictures, a destination, where I'm at in life—it's always a feeling I want to create. How I dress is an expression of my personality."
