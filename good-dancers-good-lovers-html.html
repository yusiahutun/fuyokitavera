Good dancers = good lovers

Men who know how to move on the dance floor know all the right moves in the bedroom, according to a new report.

A survey of 2,000 women has revealed that the way a man dances is a dead giveaway to his performance between the sheets.

More than 80 per cent of the women questioned said there was a definite link, and that magic movers were certain to be able to perform more than a few tricks in the bedroom.

More than half admitted to trying to get a man on to the dance floor to check out his technique before "taking the plunge".

Sex expert Flic Everett analysed the responses, taken from five UK cities, and concluded that dance floor duffers are unlikely to score highly in the passion stakes.

Flashy, over-the-top dancers are the very worst, according to 67 per cent of women. "They are so proud of their moves that they'll spend more time in bed making sure they look good rather than trying to please their partner," said Ms Everett.

But men who are shy on the dance floor are just as shy in bed according to 62 per cent.

"Beware the bloke that does the side-to-side shuffle on the dance floor. Boring, unimaginative and uninspiring, he'll generate even less energy in bed," Ms Everett added.

The study also reveals that frantic and furious "action men" who are all arms and legs on the dance floor will have plenty of energy but little imagination in bed, while those men who only dance with their mates are to be avoided - they're only likely to be interested in beer and football.

But it's good news for those who are "relaxed and rhythmic" on the dance floor. The study reveals that these dancers always look like they've got something in reserve, and they usually have.

"They'll be creative, caring, considerate, skilful and full of surprises when it comes to sex," concludes Ms Everett.

The women, aged between 18 and 40, were questioned on the streets of Glasgow, Manchester, Cardiff, Birmingham and London.

The survey was carried out by Phones 4U's to coincide launch of their latest advert, which features a man on an amusement arcade dance machine.
