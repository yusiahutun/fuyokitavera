Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift attended Patrick Mahomes’ 15 & the Mahomies foundation gala at the weekend, and the amount of adorable moments from the night are endless.

The celebrity couple walked in hand in hand, and reportedly “couldn’t stop kissing” the entire night. Travis Kelce came through with the ‘hand placement’ once again, and was just simply adorable all night. Swifties are now more than certain that he’s ‘the one’ for Taylor Swift, due to how ‘different’ their relationship seems from the rest…

Travis Kelce ‘lets Swift just be Taylor’

Taylor Swift is arguably one of the most famous celebrities at the moment, embarking on her worldwide tour and fans and paps following her every move. However, the three-time Super Bowl champion is no stranger to the spotlight himself.

Together, they’re probably one of the most-watched celeb couples at the moment, but fans see Kelce ‘look past’ the glitz and the glam, and ‘lets her just be Taylor.’

A clip of all the adorable moments of the two from the night is currently going viral on TikTok. In the one-minute montage, the pair can be seen walking hand in hand and having fun swinging handkerchiefs around at the table.

The comments are flooded with Swifties gushing over the pair, as one wrote: “I think what makes them work is he knows and appreciates that she’s ‘Taylor Swift’, but he lets her just be ‘Taylor.'”

“I really feel like she needed someone this fun and outgoing,” a second wrote, as another Swiftie replied: “That’s it, she seems so happy around him all the time.”

A third hopeful fan commented: “Every montage I see gives wedding sideshow.”

The couple could be making their red carpet debut next week

The question on everyone’s minds is, will Taylor and Travis finally be making their red-carpet debut at the Met Gala next week?

Well, the answer is, no one’s entirely sure. Although there were rumors the music star wouldn’t be attending, some sources suggest to Page Six that she will be. However, they’re unsure of Travis’ presence.

The Bad Blood singer has attended the prestigious event five times, with her last appearance being in 2016. The Kansas City Chiefs star has never attended, but there’s a first time for everything!
