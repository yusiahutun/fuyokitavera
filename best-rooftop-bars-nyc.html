30 Best Rooftop Bars In NYC

Every product on this page was chosen by a Harper's BAZAAR editor. We may earn commission on some of the items you choose to buy.

1

THE ROOF at PUBLIC Hotel

PUBLIC Hotel

This rooftop bar set against the New York City skyline seamlessly goes from a vibrant terrace for sunset drinks, to a sleek late-night spot. The communal tables and lounge areas offer multiple ways to unwind as you enjoy an evening out on the town.

The ROOF at PUBLIC Hotel, 215 Chrystie St.

2

The Fleur Room

Set on the 35th floor of the new Moxy Chelsea, this glass-encased lounge has 360-degree views of the Manhattan skyline, from the Statue of Liberty to the Empire State Building. The luxe design by Rockwell Group adds a glamorous touch to the botanical theme with rich fabrics, a copper-clad bar, and a vintage disco ball that once graced the LA club Vertigo. Perfect for both a sunset happy hour and late-night dancing, this stunning space is a must-see.

The Fleur Room, 105 W. 28th St.

3

The Crown Rooftop

Paul Carrigan

Located at Hotel 50 Bowery, The Crown Rooftop recently debuted their seasonal experience adequately titled “Royal Summer.” Take in the spirited atmosphere of the Bowery area as you sit amongst the rooftops in the airy, lush setting. During the summer months, they’ll feature music, curated art installations, and plenty of fresh cocktails.

The Crown Rooftop, 50 Bowery

4

cloudM

Located atop the citizenM Bowery Hotel, this eclectic setting features sleek decor and plenty of outdoor space to post up with friends. Take in the 360-degree views as you sip on one of their inventive cocktails.

cloudM, 189 Bowery.

5

SERRA FIORITA

Eataly USA

Set within Flatiron's Eataly, the Italian hub's rooftop bar is the perfect spot to grab a drink post-food acquiring, or stay a bit longer for a full meal. However, what really makes SERRA FIORITA stand out is their seasonal decor. It currently features vibrant florals from floor to ceiling for a the perfect garden escape.

SERRA FIORITA, 200 5th Ave.

6

Lemon's at Wythe Hotel

Wythe Hotel

A new summer pop-up bar on the roof of the Wythe Hotel, Lemon's brings a slice of the Mediterranean to Brooklyn. Enjoy a summer day sitting amongst the colorful terrace, as you enjoy tuna carpaccio with an Aperol Spritz on-tap.

Lemon's at Wythe Hotel, 80 Wythe Ave.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

7

Jimmy at the James New York

Part swanky bar, part stylish roof deck, you can sit on velvet couches amongst candlelit tables inside, or head outside to sit by the pool for grandiose views of Manhattan.

Jimmy at the James New York, 15 Thompson St.

8

Harriet’s Rooftop & Lounge

Harriet’s

Harriet's Rooftop & Lounge is part luxe cocktail bar, part green rooftop oasis. Take in the Manhattan skyline under umbrellas as you sip on expertly crafted cocktails.

Harriet’s Rooftop & Lounge, 60 Furman St.

9

Azul on the Rooftop

Bringing an Old Havana vibe to the city lights, this rooftop at the top of Hotel Hugo is a vibrant and colorful spot to sit and enjoy drinks as you take in views of Lower Manhattan. Sangria or a blood orange cosmopolitan pairs perfectly with grilled fish tacos.

Azul on the Rooftop, 525 Greenwich St.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

10

A.R.T. NoMad

Arlo NoMad Hotel

What used to be dubbed "The Heights" is now the A.R.T. NoMad, set 31 stories atop the Arlo NoMad hotel. Featuring an expansive mural from the WorldPride Mural Project that serves as an ode to New York City, it serves as the perfect backdrop to take in the city skyline and enjoy a cocktail.

A.R.T. NoMad, 11 East 31st St.

11

Cachet Boutique NYC Hotel

Located just a few blocks away from Hudson Yards, Cachet Boutique NYC Hotel is a pseudo-museum showcasing up-and-coming creatives all around the space. As you sit back and take in the views from the open-air terrace, sip of one of their specialty cocktails like 'Eden’s Paloma' made with tequila, grapefruit, and lavender.

Cachet Boutique NYC Hotel, 510 W. 42nd St.

12

Good Behavior at MADE Hotel

MADE Hotel

This 18th floor lounge within MADE Hotel offers stunning views alongside Tiki-inspired cocktails. The plush greenery and wood ceilings act as a warm retreat from the hustle and bustle, as you sip on rum drinks and dream of a beach.

Good Behavior at MADE Hotel, 44 W. 29th St.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

13

Haven Rooftop

Always changing the decor based on the season, Haven Rooftop is the perfect place to escape with a cocktail above the bustling Theater District.

Haven Rooftop, 132 W. 47th St.

14

A.R.T. SoHo

Melissa Hom/Arlo SoHo

With sweeping views of the Hudson River and signature cocktails that put a twist on the classics, you can't help but feel relaxed in this urban oasis atop the Arlo SoHo hotel. Set up at sunset under the twinkle lights and toast the night away, whether you come post-work or for some weekend fun.

A.R.T. SoHo, 231 Hudson St.

15

Mr. Purple

One of the city's ultimate hangouts, this rooftop in Hotel Indigo feels like an artist's eclectic loft and encapsulates the dynamic vibe of the Lower East Side. Post up at one of the various tables or corner couches for a dirty martini.

Mr. Purple, 180 Orchard St, 15th Floor

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

16

Ophelia

Ophelia

Located in the Beekman Tower, this rooftop speakeasy setting will transport you right back to the 1920s. Built in 1928 as a residence for working women, the history of the space is palpable, and gets a modern touch with an array of fresh cocktails.

Ophelia, The Grand Terrace at 3 Mitchel Plaza

17

Salon de Ning at the Peninsula

This enchanting terrace transports you to a more serene place as you sip on gin concoctions of mint, lychee & passion fruit while taking in spectacular views of 5th Avenue. While the city hums below, you can't help but enter a a resort state of mind.

Salon de Ning at the Peninsula, 700 5th Ave.

18

St. Cloud Social Rooftop

Courtesy St. Cloud Social Rooftop

Escape the craziness of Times Square at St. Cloud Social, a glamorous retreat at the top of the Knickerbocker Hotel. Made up of a sophisticated biergarten setting, you have the option to mingle in the crowd, or head over to your own private sky pod, as you choose from one of their three decadently-flavored margaritas.

St. Cloud Social Rooftop, 6 Times Square

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

19

The Skylark

A classic cocktail lounge with a seamless flow from indoor to outdoor, The Skylark is the ideal place to engage in elegantly-crafted libations and small plates.

The Skylark, 200 W. 39th St, 30th Floor

20

CATCH NYC

CATCH NYC

Set within the heart of Manhattan’s Meatpacking District, CATCH NYC is a stylish spot for al fresco dining. Sip on cocktails and enjoy an array of shared bites, like bourbon-miso black cod lettuce wraps and Cantonese-style lobster.

CATCH NYC, 21 9th Ave.
