Celebrity Birthdays 7 June: Liam Neeson, Emily Ratajkowski and Tom Jones

Happy Birthday to you if you're celebrating today! You share your special day with actor Liam Neeson, model Emily Ratajkowski and singing legend Tom Jones! To mark the occasion, we are taking a closer look at what is going on in the lives of these three famous faces, and how they may be celebrating as they turn one year older.

Liam Neeson turns 66

Happy Birthday to Taken star Liam Neeson, who turns 66 today! He will likely be celebrating with his two sons Michael, 22, Daniel, 21, from his marriage to the late actress Natasha Richardson, who tragically died in a skiing accident in 2009. New York-based Liam will be spending time in the UK over the summer to film a spinoff of the popular sci-fi franchise Men in Black alongside Chris Hemsworth and Tessa Thompson.

Emily Ratajkowski turns 27

It has been a momentous year for Emily Ratajkowski, who turns 27 today! In February, the model turned actress married her film producer boyfriend Sebastian Bear-McClard in a surprise ceremony at New York Town Hall. Also in her 26th year, the brunette beauty launched her very own line of swimwear called Inamorata. We can't wait to see what the next year has in store for Emily!

Tom Jones turns 78

It will be a busy 78th birthday for legendary Welsh singer Tom Jones. Having toured the USA in the lead up to his big day, the It's Not Unusual singer will embark on his European leg on 21st June, kicking off with a performance at Hampton Court Festival. Tom, who is a coach on singing competition The Voice, mentored this year's winner, 21-year-old Ruti Olajugbagbe.
