Back to Business NewBiz grants help businesses launched during pandemic

published on January 31, 2024
A new program from the state of Illinois is helping businesses that launched during the COVID-19 pandemic recoup some of their losses. Governor JB Pritzker and the Illinois Department of Commerce and Economic Opportunity (DCEO) recently announced $25 million in grant funding through the Back to Business (B2B) NewBiz program. The program is designed to provide additional support for businesses in hard-hit sectors that launched during the pandemic. The businesses that launched and remained in operation faced major impacts during the pandemic but have not been eligible for other federal or state relief programs since they were not open and running before the pandemic.
