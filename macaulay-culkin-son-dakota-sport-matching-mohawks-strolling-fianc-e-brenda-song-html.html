Macaulay Culkin and son Dakota sport matching mohawks while strolling with fiance Brenda Song

Macaulay Culkin looked blissful as he enjoyed a walk around a residential neighborhood in Los Angeles on Monday with his family.

The 41-year-old actor was looking cool and casual while sporting a wide mohawk hairdo that his 11-month-old son Dakota adorably matched.

The cute father–son duo were joined in their stroll by Macaulay's partner of five years, actress Brenda Song, 34.

The family's outing comes just over two months after Macaulay and Brenda were reported to be engaged.

A source told People in late January that the lovebirds had gotten engaged, and Brenda seemingly confirmed the news shortly afterward when she was spotted out in public wearing a diamond ring on her left ring finger.

The couple, who have been dating since 2017, had previously welcomed their son Dakota in early April 2021.

Although it hasn't been long since his birth, a source told Us Weekly last month that the new parents are already looking forward to further expanding their family. 

'They want more than one kid for sure. … It can definitely happen soon,' they claimed, adding that it wouldn't be a 'surprise' if the two conceived another child after Dakota's first birthday.
