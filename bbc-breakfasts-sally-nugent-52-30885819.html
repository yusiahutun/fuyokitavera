BBC Breakfasts Sally Nugent, 52, flashes legs in racy thigh-split dress as fans go wild

BBC Breakfast beauty Sally Nugent had fans swooning at their screens after she paraded her jaw-dropping legs in a gorgeous gown.

The presenter, 52, was one of many celebs in attendance at the glitzy National Television Awards on Tuesday evening (September 5).

Hosted by Joel Dommett from London’s O2, the fancy ceremony celebrates the UK's biggest shows and much-loved TV talent.

Sharing a slew of sizzling snaps to her Instagram on Wednesday (September 6), the TV favourite oozed beauty and style in a rather revealing dress.

Dressed up to the nines, Sally looked red hot as she slipped her ageless figure into the gorgeous slashed bright red gown.
