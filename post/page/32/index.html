The meaning of Laura by Giorgione

published on September 21, 2024
Laura by Giorgione is a masterpiece of the Italian Renaissance, painted around 1506. The painting depicts a young woman believed to be Laura, the beloved of the famous poet Petrarch. The half-length portrait captures Laura’s enigmatic and serene expression, with her gaze directed off into the distance. The rich colors and soft brushwork typical of Giorgione’s style enhance the sense of mystery and contemplation in the artwork.
