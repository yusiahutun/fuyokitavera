Iowa Goodwill stores reopen to shoppers Friday

published on September 27, 2024
After reopening its Clinton location on May 1, Goodwill of the Heartland announced 11 more of its Iowa retail stores will reopen to shoppers on Friday. This includes stores located in Bettendorf, Davenport, Muscatine and Burlington. The nonprofit retailer says hours of operation for donating and shopping will be reduced to 11 a.m. to 6 p.m. Monday through Saturday and noon to 5 p.m. Sunday until further notice.
