10 Summer Shih Tzu haircuts for those who love grooming their pets

Shih Tzus are more than pets in your home; they are another family member. That’s why it’s important to spend some time grooming them. Since their fur grows a lot, it might make them uncomfortable, especially during summer. Adorable summer Shih Tzu haircuts can help avoid this, making your dog feel loved and safe in your hands.

Should Shih Tzu be shaved in summer? As the hot temperature season rolls around, giving your furry friend a summer Shih Tzu haircut isn't just about helping them beat the heat; it's also a fun way to highlight their unique personality.

Best summer Shih Tzu haircut

What is a summer cut for a Shih Tzu? Summer Shih Tzu haircuts are all about balancing style with comfort. A good trim can help your furry friend stay cool and prevent their coat from becoming tangled as the temperature rises.

Many styles exist, from the classic puppy cut to the more daring lion cut. Each haircut offers a unique look, allowing your Shih Tzu to strut their stuff while enjoying the summer season. The following cuts will showcase your pet's character:

1. Teddy bear cut

The teddy bear cut Shih Tzu is an adorable grooming style that transforms your pup into a cuddly bear. The body hair is trimmed to a short length of about 2-3 inches, giving a fluffy and rounded appearance.

The hair on the face and ears is left slightly longer and shaped into a round look to resemble a teddy bear's face. Regular grooming and brushing are required to keep this style neat, fluffy, and cool.

2. Puppy cut

The Shih Tzu haircut for a puppy is the most common among Shih Tzu haircuts. It's simple to execute and ideal for hot summer days. This style, also known as a Summer cut, is the best haircut for a Shih Tzu to keep it cool and comfortable during the hot months.

It's simpler to do than most other Shih Tzu hairstyles. In this summer haircut, the body hair is cut short to about one inch, while the hair on the face is left a tad longer. However, it's still short enough to ensure it doesn't fall into your pup's sensitive eyes.

3. Lion cut

There was a time you dreamed of having a lion at home but prioritized safety. Now, you can get the best of both worlds! The lion cut turns your Shih Tzu into something super adorable. It's a great hairstyle that highlights their cute faces.

Similar to the puppy cut, the fur on the body is trimmed short to about one to two inches. However, the hair around the face and head is cut to resemble a lion's mane. It's quite easy to maintain, making it a perfect haircut style for summer weather.

4. Long ear puppy cut

This cut is similar to the Puppy Cut, except the hair on the ears is long. This cut is still the best for summer. The long-ear puppy cut has a softer feminine appearance.

5. Schnauzer cut

The Schnauzer Shih Tzu haircut is a distinctive style that gives your Shih Tzu a unique and dignified look. This cut is named after the Schnauzer dog breed because it mimics their grooming style. The body hair is trimmed short, while the hair on the legs, chest, and face is left long.

One of the defining features of the Schnauzer haircut for a male Shih Tzu is the distinct beard, achieved by leaving the hair on the muzzle longer. Such features give your Shih Tzu a cute and distinguished appearance.

6. Practical top knot

The practical top knot is a popular Shih Tzu hairstyle focusing only on the dog's head, leaving the rest of the coat unchanged. This style is common in both summer and winter. In this haircut, the hair on the head is kept long and tied up with a bow, allowing the hair to fall gracefully.

Not only is this style visually appealing, but it's also practical. It keeps the hair away from the Shih Tzu's eyes and mouth, preventing discomfort. The top knot style also allows you to add cute accessories like bows or bands, giving your Shih Tzu a personalized and stylish look.

7. Shih Tzu haircut with pigtails

For Lady Shih Tzus, the Pigtail haircut is a total cuteness overload. While the usual go-to is a ponytail, opting for pigtails takes their adorableness to a new level. Not to mention, it's a playful twist that also makes it super easy to tell their gender.

So, to add beauty to your female Shih Tzu's look, why not give the delightful pigtail style a whirl? It's sure to turn heads and melt hearts wherever she goes.

8. Top knot show cut

The top knot show cut is a beautiful style. It features long hair extending from your dog's back to its feet. Regular grooming and trimming are key for this cut, which requires multiple brushings daily. It's mostly seen on show dogs.

9. Short cut

Do you own a Shih Tzu but have a hectic schedule that leaves no time for coat maintenance? Or do you reside in hot areas? Fear not—the extra-short Shih Tzu haircut is your solution for the summer. This style is perfect for keeping your pup comfortable in high temperatures.

It also eliminates the need for regular brushing and detangling, saving you from the worry of matted fur. So, opt for this fuss-free hairstyle and let your Shih Tzu enjoy the summer without discomfort.

10. Mohawk cut

The mohawk Shih Tzu haircut is a fun and edgy style that will make your pup stand out. As the name suggests, this haircut has a strip of hair moving from the forehead to the back, similar to a mohawk in human hairstyles.

The rest of the body is trimmed short, making Mohawk the main focus. Brushing is needed to keep the longer hair neat and prevent tangles.

Wrapping up

A haircut for your Shih Tzu keeps them cool during the hot months and gives them a fresh and stylish look. From the practical puppy cut to the adventurous lion cut, summer Shih Tzu haircuts suit every pet and owner's preferences. Grooming your pet's coat is necessary to maintain its beauty and health.
