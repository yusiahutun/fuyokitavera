Who is Dj Fatxo? Controversies, music, family, background

The Kenyan music scene has developed from what it was over five years ago. The industry is now witnessing new talent, with stars emerging from various genres. One such star is Dj Fatxo, a singer, songwriter and Dj specialising in secular music, more so, Mugithi. However, the upcoming star has recently been in the spotlight for various negative reasons. So, what happened to him? And who is Dj Fatxo?

In a short time, he has progressed from a TV deejay to becoming one of Kenya's most celebrated Mugithi artists. However, his journey has been challenging. What happened to Dj Fatxo?

The Mugithi singer is at the centre of a controversy after a young interior designer named Geoffrey "Jeff" Mwathi died while visiting him in Nairobi, Kenya.

According to the police, a 23-year-old male died after falling from the tenth floor of Redwood Apartments in the Kasarani area, Nairobi. The initial report stated that Jeff committed su*cide, but his family has denied this. The hom*cide unit of the Director of Criminal Investigation (DCI) is still investigating the incident.

Who is Dj Fatxo?

Dj Fatxo is a Kenyan DJ and musician. He rose to fame for his catchy Kikuyu secular songs, making him a fan favourite. He enjoys a huge fanbase and is among the upcoming young celebrities in Kenya.

Lawrence Njuguna Wagura, also known by his stage name Dj Fatxo was born on 25 April 1996 in Kinangop, Engineer, Nyandarua County, Kenya. He is the only child in his family.

Lawrence attended various primary schools in his home area before high school. During an interview on The Jeff Kuria Podcast on 19 May 2021, the musician revealed that he attended, Kingapo pride, Morning Starlight Academy and Bridges view primary schools.

Who are Dj Fatxo's parents?

His parents are Mr Wagura and Mrs Njoki. On 14 February 2022, DJ Fatxo surprised his parents with a fully furnished house. The star praised his parents on social media, thanking them for inspiring his success.

What does Dj Fatxo do?

The celebrity is involved in various ventures, including being a Dj, a musician and an entrepreneur. Here is a breakdown of all his career ventures.

Dee Jaying

Lawrence began his career as a disk jockey in 2015 after enrolling in DJ Mo's System Unit DJ Academy. After graduating, he started deejaying in local clubs and events, gaining recognition. Among Fatxo's spots was in Ruiru, where he hosted small-time gigs in various establishments. However, DJ Fatxo got his big break at Kenyatta University's broadcasting television (KU TV), where he was their resident disk jockey.

Music

After gaining popularity with the Sailors Gang, DJ Fatxo decided to pursue a career in music. He released his first single, "Tuinuke Twi Kong'o," on Youtube on 6 January 2020, following it up with "Kiogora" on 17 February 2020 and "Ndi Mang'a" on 18 April 2020. The following are DJ Fatxo's songs:

Entrepreneurship

DJ Fatxo is a successful businessman and entrepreneur. He serves as the CEO of Dope Unit Entertainment, which he founded sometime in 2017/2018. His business also provides branding, event management/planning, artist management, and DJ services. The well-known DJ also manages a school where he instructs aspiring disc jockeys.

Who is DJ Fatxo's girlfriend?

DJ Fatxo's girlfriend is Gathoni Waruguru, a TV presenter at Nyumba Iitu TV, a local vernacular station. The celebrity recently posted his girlfriend on his Instagram, celebrating her birthday on 5 March 2023.

In 2022, the musician made news after allegedly gifting his wife (now ex-wife) a new car.
