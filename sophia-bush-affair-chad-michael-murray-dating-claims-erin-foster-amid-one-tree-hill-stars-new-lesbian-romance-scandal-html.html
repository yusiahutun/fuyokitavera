Sophia Bush had AFFAIR with Chad Michael Murray while we were dating, claims Erin Foster... amid One

Erin Foster has come forward to claim that her then-boyfriend Chad Michael Murray cheated on her with his One Tree Hill co-star Sophia Bush.

During a conversation with her sister Sara on The World's First Podcast, the actress, 41 — who dated the actor from 2001 to 2002 — brought up the alleged affair after a fan wrote in and asked them to share 'good' dating stories.

Erin then referenced a post previously made by the gossip page DeuxMoi that said Murray 'apparently cheated on her' with Bush — only to confirm it.

'I mean, yeah. He definitely did cheat on me with her, in like, a pretty egregious way,' she claimed. 

The daughter of legendary musician David Foster added: 'While we were living together. Pretty cool.' 

Erin's revelation comes amid Bush's new romance with women's soccer star Ashlyn Harris, 39 — whose recent ex-wife Ali Krieger seemingly confirmed that Harris cheated on her with Bush before filing for divorce in mid-September.

It was rumored at the time that Murray had stepped out on their marriage with none other than his House of Wax co-star Paris Hilton

Bush attempted to get the marriage annulled on her grounds of fraud, but her efforts were unsuccessful, and the divorce was ultimately finalized in 2006.

'This is a difficult and unfortunate situation. I am glad this is being resolved sooner rather than later,' she said in a statement shared with PEOPLE at the time of the finalization. 

Recounting their relationship in 2014, she said on Watch What Happens Live:  'We were two stupid kids who had no business being in a relationship in the first place.'

'To all the other costars who've worked it out, more power to you.'

'After being friends for years, and running in the same social circles, Sophia and Ashlyn went out on their first dinner date a couple of weeks ago,' a source told People on Tuesday.
