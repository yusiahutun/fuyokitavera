Who is India Eisley Dating? Has Place For Boyfriend In A Booming Career

EntertainmentBy LRU Writer | On: February 14, 2018

Although the term 'dating' may seem common, it also may not be someone's forte, as it is not very easy to maintain a relationship with someone while being surrounded by a busy career. 

Talking about the beautiful actress, India Eisley, does she have a place for romance in her life although she runs a busy professional life? Let's dig in to find out! 

Is India Eisley Dating Secret Boyfriend? 

With no doubt, actress India Eisley lands on the spot in the list of the gorgeous actresses in Hollywood. With her beautiful appearance, India has gained tons of followers along the way in her career who wishes to find out the real deal about her relationship status.

But, even though her fans wish to figure out about her dating life, India is one of those many celebrities who prefer to remain low-key with minimal clues surfacing the media.

However, back in the year 2009, in a short interview, India disclosed that she never had a boyfriend. 

India Eisley talks on boyfriend in an interview (Published on 10 August 2009) 

However, it's almost a decade since the interview, but there still is no traces of her dating life with a possible boyfriend. 

So, as much as India's fans may feel happy hearing that she is single in the eyes of the public, there still are possible chances that she is enjoying her love life away from the spotlight. As a result, we might just have to wait until India is more open on this matter. 

A Bit More About India Eisley:

Actress India Eisley was born on October 29, in 1993 in Los Angeles, California and goes by the full name, India Joy Eisley. She was born to parents, David Glen Eisley, a musician, and Olivia Hussey, an actress. Additionally, in the family, India also has a half-brother, Max.

Talking about her career, in the year 2005, India appeared in the movie, 'Headspace,' when she was just twelve years old. Furthermore, she continued working in minor roles until she got her big break with a significant role in 'The Secret Life of the American Teenager' in the year 2008. 

India appeared as Sawa in the action movie, 'Kite' in the year 2014 along with actor Samuel L. Jackson. Towards the end of 2014, she also filmed 'Social Suicide' in the year 2015 which was a modern take on the classic story of Romeo and Juliet. Later in 2015, India also filmed the dark fantasy movie, 'The Curse of Sleeping Beauty' as Briar Rose. 

In 2016, she also appeared in the movie, 'Adolescence' and even shared her excitement via Instagram. 

However, despite having multiple acting credits to her name, India is widely-known for her role as Eve in the 2012 movie, 'Underworld: Awakening.' Moreover, despite a busy schedule, the beautiful actress has been able to stay fit and in shape recently, in comparison to a few years ago. As a result, her fans are waiting for all the scoop on her weight loss as well as workout tips.
