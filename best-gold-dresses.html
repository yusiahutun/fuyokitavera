10 best gold dresses for the special event in your diary

Meghan Markle has proven that gold dresses aren't just for party season, dazzling fans with her glam strapless midi as she attended the Ms. Foundation Women of Vision Awards alongside Prince Harry. 

The Duchess looked incredible in her glittering Johanna Ortiz gold dress, which she teamed with a pair of Tom Ford Lizard-Print Heeled Sandals and Carolina Herrara Metropolitan Insignia’ Small Box Bag in Yellow. 

If you're feeling inspired by Meghan's showstopping ensemble, look no further, we've searched high and low for the best gold dresses that will help you to channel the Duchess this spring, from glitzy maxi dresses to slinky midi dresses and more. 

Best gold dresses for women

  • Revolve Norma Kavali TY Front NK Shirt Mini Dress

    This elegant gold dress features a tie at the front which cinches at the waist for a lovely silhouette. It's short in a fun and flirty way and reviews are glowing, with several shoppers saying they're "obsessed" and have never had so many compliments over a dress.

  • Rixo Claudette Gold Bandana Dress

    If you're a fan of the Thirties-inspired look, the glam Claudette gold dress is the one for you. It's made of lightweight gold crepe and features a flattering ruched bodice and detachable straps and conceals a strapless bra.

  • Anthropologie Queens of Archive Romy One-Shoulder Mini Dress

    Talk about show-stopping! This gorgeous gold gown is a real attention-grabbing piece, featuring a one-shoulder puff-sleeved design and a thigh-skimming mini skirt and zipper closure.

  • Brandalley Herve Leger Gold Cap Sleeve Foil Bandage Dress

    This figure-hugging gold mini dress is cut from Herve Leger's signature bandage and features a faux crossover bust, sweetheart neckline, and cap sleeves. Gorgeous, and it's a real bargain too!

  • ASOS Miss Selfridge Embellished Sequin Tiered Mini Dress

    Looking for a design that combines gold, sequins and boho style? Then look no further! This dress has a pretty tiered skirt, v-neck and a button keyhole back. It's available in sizes 4-16.

  • Warehouse Gold Fluid Lamé Maxi Dress

    Kitted out in flowing gold lamé, you'll really feel like the belle of the ball. This gold maxi dress, which features spaghetti straps, would be great styled with either heels or boots and a rockstar belt.

  • New Look Goddiva Gold Bandeau Tiered Hem Maxi Dress

    We're getting definite mermaid vibes from this stunning tiered hem maxi dress from New Look. It's made from comfy stretch jersey fabric for a figure-hugging bodycon fit. Pair with some statement earrings for a real VIP look.

  • Bronx and Banco Gold Florence Strapless Gown

    Bronx and Banco's ultra-glam strapless gold dress features a cinched fitted bodice and a pleated skirt, complete with a feminine side slit. The maxi dress would make the perfect investment piece for very specia occasions, and the gold frock would look amazing teamed with a pair of strappy stiletto heels.

  • Selfridges The Vampire's Wife Mettalic Midnight Woven Dress

    A real investment piece, The Vampire's Wife's gold creation is a dress that will surely stand the test of time. It has gorgeous fluttery sleeves, and a statuesque silhouette stitched with metallic thread that shimmers and flickers all the way to a thigh-grazing, flared skirt.  

  • Club London Anya Sequin Gold Dress

    Shimmering champagne gold sequins cover the fabric of this chic asymmetric dress, which features a draped cowl neckline and delicate cami straps which cross over the back and lace up for a high-impact design. The modest hemline is balanced with a daring thigh split and flattering ruching on the curves.
