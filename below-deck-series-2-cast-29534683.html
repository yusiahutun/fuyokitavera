Below Deck series 2 cast now - solo pregnancy, career U-turn, rival reality show

The hit Bravo TV show Below Deck premiered in 2013 and in its ten long seasons, we have seen a huge turnover in the cast.

The reality show follows the lives of young crew members who work and reside aboard lavish superyachts during charter season.

The show has massively changed since its first season but with extremely rich yacht owners to please, it's never short of drama.

READ MORE: Below Deck series 1 cast now - assault arrest, bartending and 'living the dream'

Crew members have come and gone and fans have been forced to say goodbye to some beloved characters.

Daily Star has taken a look at where your favorite crew members from season two have shipped off to after the show.

Ben Robinson

It's safe to say that Ben was a fan-favourite in the second season.

He was the chef on the boat and fans will be happy to hear he kept up his incredible cooking after the show.

According to ScreenRant, Ben still works as a chef, is featured on YouTube tutorials and hosts classes over Zoom, where he shows participants how to make the dishes he served on the show.

Kate Chastain

Florida beauty Kate Chastain worked as the Chief Stewardess and stayed in the show for six seasons until she left in 2020.

Announcing her exit, she wrote: "I intend to step back as a senior member of the Below Deck Family and work to become financially independent while continuing to fully support His Majesty, Andy Cohen."

Screen Rant reported that after leaving Below Deck, Kate continued to work on the reality show by co-hosting and working as an executive producer on Bravo Chat Room.

That's not all, because she recently appeared on reality show The Traitors US.

And she's due to become a first-time mum as her baby is due in May, and she's planning on raising the bundle solo.

She told PEOPLE Magazine that she's "thrilled to become a mother."

Kat Held

Who can forget Kat Held, the crew member that just always wanted to be at the bar?

According to Screen Rant, she experimented with different careers and went back home to Rhode Island. Kat worked as a waitress and eventually enrolled in nursing school in 2017.

She moved out west to Las Vegas, where she currently works as a bartender.

Amy Johnson

Amy used to be the third stewardess on the show during the second season.

She left the show and met the love of her life, because she married her boyfriend Ellis Bayside in October 2020.

Her brother Kelley was there and helped take pictures of the wedding.

She has worked as a wedding planner but returned to TV when she appeared in Below Deck Galley Talk.

Eddie Lucas

Eddie is a face that we definitely recognised as he appeared in seasons one two, three, eight, and nine.

The star had to take a break following his affair with Raquel 'Rocky' Dakota during season three.

He made a return to Below Deck in series eight and nine and was promoted to a First Officer.

Excitingly, Eddie is now living his lifelong dream as the captain of a harbor tug in Baltimore Harbor.

Kelley Johnson

Kelley Johnson, Amy's brother, used to be the second engineer and had a passion for fishing and photography.

The star is now following his dream and is a photographer, so snapping his sister Amy's wedding must have been a special moment for them both.
