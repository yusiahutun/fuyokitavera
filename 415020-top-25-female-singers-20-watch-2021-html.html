Top 25 female singers under 25 to watch out for in 2021

The 21st century has seen the rise of stars in the music industry. Technology has given artists more advantage and made things more convenient whenever they want to release their art. Female singers under 25 are killing it in their respective genres and commanding huge followings, some even bigger than the musicians they look up to.

Music is therapy for many, and that is why female pop singers get to release singles, EPs, and albums after a while to cater for their demanding audiences. Famous girl singers like Miley Cyrus, Katy Perry, Taylor Swift, Arianna Grande and Selena Gomez continue to guide the new talents in the industry.

Female singers under 25

Young singers are always a “taste to sore ears” in any form of music. They bring fresh music, creativity, and competition between the young and old artistes each year. Here are 25 female musicians that are under 25 as of the year 2021:

1. Billy Eilish – 19 years old

2015 will always remain a favourite year for Billy Eilish as it is the year the teen superstar came to the limelight. Her debut single, Ocean Eyes, which her brother interestingly wrote, became a Soundcloud hit, and that is when her star started to shine. Today, Billy Eilish has four albums and EPs to her name.

2. Nova Miller – 19 years old

Swedish Star Nova Miller is one of the young female musicians everyone needs to watch out for. The 19-year-old is signed to Universal Music Group and is known for her songwriting, dancing skills, acting and being a multi-instrumentalist. Popular songs by the young artiste include Turn Up the Fire, My Perfect Christmas, and Not Your Number.

3. Grace Vanderwaal – 17 years old

Grace Vanderwaal is among the many success stories that have come out thanks to YouTube. She is among singers under 18. The teen started her career by posting musical covers, and that is where her fans noticed her distinct vocals.

4. Lydia Night – 20 years old

Lydia Night is the lead vocalist and rhythm guitarist of the California punk rock band; The Regrettes. Before joining her current band, Lydia was the lead vocalist and guitarist of pop-rock bands Pretty Little Demons and LILA and a frequent collaborator with the rock duo Dead Man's Bones. Popular songs by the artiste include Dare You, Seashore, and California Friends.

5. Mikaela Astel – 15 years old

The singer and songwriter from New York City has proved that you can be young, still in school, and work on your talents simultaneously. 15-year-old Mikaela Astel was one of the stars on The Voice 2019 and has continued to show her singing prowess even after the talent show.

6. Noah Cyrus – 21 years old

Noah Cyrus is the younger sister of pop star Miley Cyrus. Despite a 7-year gap between the two sisters, both have worked together to produce amazing music for their fans. Noah Cyrus has four albums and EPs to her name. Some of her most loved songs include Make Me, Dear August, All Three, and The Worst of You.

7. Emily Bear – 19 years old

Emily Bear is a singer, composer and Pianist from Illinois. The teen is gradually making a name by establishing herself as one of the finest young classical, jazz, and film score singers of her generation. The musical prodigy has so far released eight albums. She is one of the most promising female music artists.

8. Annika Rose – 19 years old

19-year-old Annika Rose is known for bangers like Talk to Strangers, Just Like You, Still Writing Songs About You, and Story of My Life. Annika Rose was born into a musical family. It was no surprise when she chose a career in music.

9. Tate McRae – 17 years old

Tate Rosner McRae is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and dancer. Tate first became famous when she became the first Canadian finalist on the American reality TV show So You Think You Can Dance. She was only 13. Tate's star has been shining since then, and she has gone ahead to release bangers like 'You Broke Me First', 'Lie to Me', and 'Wish I Loved You in the 90's'.

10. Azalyne – 13 years old

At just 13 years old, the singer and songwriter has become a favourite among teens. She is one of the youngest female singers in her city. The young musician also plays the piano, guitar, electric guitar, and bass guitar.

Azalyne has been performing as a solo artist and with local bands since she was 6. Popular songs by the 13-year-old include You Are Mighty, Stalker, and E-Girl.

11. Beabadoobee​ – 20 years old

Born Beatrice Laus, the Filipino-British singer-songwriter, is taking the industry by storm. The bubbly singer released four extended plays under the independent label Dirty Hit: Lice, Patched Up, Loveworm and Space Cade between 2018 and 2019. Beabadoobee's debut album, Fake It Flowers, was positively received by fans and continues to top the charts since 2020.

12. Mxmtoom – 19 years old

Mxmtoom is one of the savviest Indie-pop singers of her generation. The Oakland California native is trying to make it big in the competitive industry like many of her peers. Mxmtoom songs that have banged include Walk but in a Garden, Prom Dress, Falling For You, and Unspoken Words.

13. Halle Bailey – 20 years old

It is impossible not to love Halle Bailey. The young musician is talented and always has something to offer to her fans. Together with her older sister Chloe, Halle has been making music, producing and even got into acting.

Lola Young tries to make her music about modern womanhood. The British star makes authentic music while still trying to be as big as her influences. She draws her inspiration from musicians like Prince, Frank Ocean and Joni Mitchell.

15. Bulow – 20 years old

Canadian pop singer Bulow is one to watch out for. She is one of the youngest popular singers with a huge following on digital streaming platforms. Bhad Bhabie became famous after a clip of her on Dr Phil saying, "catch me outside, how about that?" became a viral internet meme. 17. Tiffany Day – 19 years old

Tiffany Day became an internet sensation in 2017 when a video of her singing 'Hallelujah' went viral. The video was shared on several social media platforms and even earned her a couple of media interviews.

Myla Finks is one half of the Atlanta duo Hello Sunday. Myla Fiink's high note and distinct vocals are what make her an interesting singer to follow. The 15-year-old first came into the limelight after participating in The Voice 2019 and has been one of the most favourite participants.

19. Chelsea Glover – 15 years old

Chelsea Glover is the second half of the Atlanta duo Hello Sunday. Chelsea and Myla Finks are friends whom everyone wants to perform together. The 15-year-old budding musician has been singing since she was a child.

The Voice is not the first huge platform where she performed. Chelsea has also previously auditioned for School of Rock on Broadway in New York City.

20. Carlie Hanson – 20 years old

CarlieHanson's Only One is the song that brought her to the limelight. The American singer and songwriter released more hits that include Gucci Knife, Good Enough, Daze Inn, and WYA. Carlie Hanson is signed to Warner Bros and is one of the most promising pop and alt-pop musicians today.

21. Olivia O' Brien – 20 years old

The Californian native always means business when she is handed over the mic. Olivia O'Brien became famous in 2016 after collaborating with Gnash on the single I Hate U, I Love U. She has made hits like Just a Boy, Find What You Are Looking For, and Care Less More.

22. Griff – 20 years old

The 20-year-old British pop star is growing to be one of the high charting UK pop musicians. Apart from releasing hits and bangers, Griff has numerous awards, including a Brit Awards for Rising star award. Renown songs by her include Black Hole, Inside Out, Sound of your Voice, and Eternal Flame. She is among the most adored British female singers by teenagers in the UK.

23. Olivia Rodrigo – 18 years old

Teen actress and musician Olivia Rodrigo is great both on-screen and in the studio. Most of her fans know her as Paige Olvera on the Disney Channel series Bizaardvark and Nini Salazar-Roberts on the Disney+ series High School Musical. What some do not know is that she also sings and sings well. You can check out her songs that include Driver's Licence, Good For You, and Traitor.

Mackenzie Frances Ziegler sings, dances, writes and acts. 25. Maisie Peters – 19 years old

British singer and songwriter Maisie Peters is one of the entertainers who will forever be grateful for YouTube. The social media site gave her a platform where she could share her talent, and she has been getting better since she started singing in the 2010s. Her debut single, Place We Were Made is still a banger.

The top female singers under 25 have realized how powerful social media is, and that is why you will find them constantly growing their platforms by posting content that their followers want.
