Taron Egerton

Taron Egerton- Biography

Taron Egerton is a Welsh actor and singer who has been working in the show business since 2011. He began his acting career on stage before moving on to parts in films and television programs. He is most recognized for his major role in ‘The Kingsman: The Secret Service,’ a 2014 action spy comedy film. At the start of his acting career, he had numerous challenges and setbacks. He is currently regarded as one of the top actors working today. Aside from that, in 2015 and 2019, he appeared in two music videos with two separate musicians.

What is the Net Worth of Taron Egerton? Salary, Earnings

Egerton is recognized as a rising star in Hollywood. Egerton’s net worth is expected to be $6 million in 2022.

Taron Egerton- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings

Taron David Egerton was born in Birkenhead, Merseyside, England on November 10, 1989. His father ran a bed and breakfast and his mother is a social worker. Despite being born in England, he considers himself Welsh and speaks with a Welsh accent. He was also born under the sign of Scorpio and is 32 years old.

Education

Taron Egerton completed his studies at West Wales’ Ysgol Penglais Comprehensive School. He continued his education at the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art (RADA). Similarly, in 2012, he received a BA (Hons) in Acting.

Taron Egerton- Relationship, Girlfriend

From 2016 to 2018, Taron was in a relationship with Emily Thomas. He is currently unmarried and unattached.

Taron Egerton- Professional Career

Taron engaged in acting classes because he was interested in the craft. He was also selected Student of the Year by the Stephen Sondheim Society in 2011. In addition, he appeared in several theater performances, including ‘Beauty and the Beast’ in 2006, ‘The House of Special Purpose’ in 2011, and ‘The Last of the Hausmanns’ in 2012.

Taron made his television debut in 2013 as Liam Jay in ‘Lewis.’ He played Dennis Severs in ‘The Smoke’ in 2014. Similarly, he was cast as a voice artist in ‘Watership Down,’ a 2018 adventure fantasy miniseries. Similarly, he voiced Moomintroll in 26 episodes of Moominvalley from 2019 to 2020. He made an appearance in the 2019 series ‘The Dark Crystal: Age of Resistance.’ In this series, he also gave the voice to the character Rian. He also appeared alongside Anya Taylor-Joy, Nathalie Emmanuel, Harris Dickinson, Helena Bonham Carter, and many others on the big screen.

Taron Egerton’s first two feature roles were in the 2012 short films Pop’ and ‘Hereafter’. In 2014, he had his first substantial part on the big screen. In the drama film ‘Testament of Youth,’ he played one of the main characters, Edward Brittain. Similarly, when he was cast in the 2015 action spy comedy picture ‘Kingsman: The Secret Service,’ this actor shot to notoriety. The film was lauded by critics and earned the ‘Empire Award for Best British Film. Taron’s popularity skyrocketed as a result of the commercial success of this picture. He played Edward Smith in ‘Legend’ the same year. Similarly, in 2016, he appeared as a voice actor in ‘Sing,’ where he voiced Johnny. Matthew McConaughey, Reese Witherspoon, Scarlett Johansson, and John C. Reilly also appeared in the film.

Body Measurements: Height, Weight, Hair color

Taron Egerton is of White origin and British nationality. Aside from that, he is 5 feet and 9 inches (1.75m/175cm) tall and weighs around 78kgs (172lbs). He also has an athletic body type, green eyes, and brown hair.
