Lupita Nyong'o's Space-Saving Floating Staircase Impresses Interior Designers: &quot;Star of The Show&quot;

  • Actress Lupita Nyong'o showed off her stunning floating staircase in a dance video to Kill The Lights
  • Lupita's floating staircase caught the eyes of accomplished interior designers such as Mohammed Ahmed and Jenifer Ebert, who said they made her living room brighter and larger
  • The interior designers also raved about Lupita's sleek and modern floating staircase design

Actress Lupita Nyong'o is the talk of the town, and this time, it is not because of her on-screen talents but because of her beautiful house

Lupita's staircase descends into the living room

The beautiful actress posted a video of herself dancing to Kill The Lights (Mix Cut) (Audien Remix) by Alex Newell, Jess Glynne & DJ Cassidy; apart from its lyrics and her beauty, people also noticed the staircase.

The staircase, which descended directly into the living room, was the star of the show in Lupita's living room, and interior designers are raving about it.

Basically, it is a floating staircase in light natural wood. Lupita's stairs are edged with dark wood pieces and accented by a chrome metal railing. The spaces between the stairs reveal a glimpse of the art on Lupita's white walls, her sofa, and her wooden side table.

Homes and Gardens reports that the staircase idea is a favourite among interior designers, mainly if used in small spaces. According to the experts, the floating staircase has beautiful aesthetic benefits for the living room.

Ahmed says they offer sleek, modern design

Mohammad Ahmed, interior designer at The Home Guidance, said, "Floating stairs offer a sleek, modern design that can become a focal point of the room. They can be customized with a variety of materials and designs to fit the specific style of any home."

Jennifer Ebert, Digital Editor at Homes & Gardens, said that floating staircases can amplify the beauty of the rest of your design.

"The open design of floating staircases allows the more intentional elements of your design to shine. In Lupita Nyong'o's living room, the floating staircase allows guests to see her wall art from all angles of the room."

The home experts also revealed that the floating staircase can make a room appear brighter and larger.

"Traditional staircases, with their risers and stringers, can create visual obstructions. Floating stairs, on the other hand, have an open design that removes these obstructions, making the space look more open and expansive," he said. "The materials often used in floating stair designs, whether it's polished wood or metal, can reflect light, adding to the room's luminosity," he continued.

Ebert also said that the open stairs between stairs allow light to flow through rather than blocking it.
