Very arresting! The ten sexiest POLICE UNIFORMS revealed, with Texas, New Hampshire and Missouri all

America's ten sexiest police uniforms have been unveiled, with the Lone Star state of Texas claiming the top spot, while New Jersey and Missouri secured high rankings.

A poll conducted by Wealth of Geeks sought answers from 3,000 individuals to find the most attractive state police uniforms across the country. 

An element that distinguishes Texas state troopers from the rest is their iconic Cowboy hats, described by the website as embodying 'the swagger of a rodeo champion and the solemn duty of a ranger.' 

Following closely behind is New Hampshire's crisp, stately forest green shirts paired with tan trousers, symbolizing 'the state's vast wilderness and exuding the welcoming charm of the small towns nestled within.'

1. Texas Department of Public Safety

Texas state troopers' 'Texas Tan' shirts have claimed the prestigious No. 1 spot in the ranking of the sexiest state police uniforms. 

According to the website, these shirts in a crisp, authoritative dark tan not only command authority but also serve as a banner of pride and tradition.

The ranking says these shirts go beyond being mere attire. 'They're a banner of pride and tradition, tailored to stand tall against the backdrop of bustling cities, sprawling ranches, and everything in between.'

2. New Hampshire State Police

Securing the runner-up position is the uniform of the New Hampshire State Police, described as 'as enduring as the state's vast wilderness and as welcoming as the small towns nestled within.'

New Hampshire's law enforcement officers wear shirts in a crisp, stately forest green, paired with tan trousers. 

The ensemble is completed by a belt that symbolizes the strength inherent in New England character.

The uniform serves as more than just clothing; it's a nod to the commitment and courage essential for upholding the law in a land where the state's motto, 'Live Free or Die,' as noted by the website.

 3. Missouri State Highway Patrol

The Missouri State Highway Patrol secures the third position in the ranking with its distinguished light blue shirts. 

Drawing inspiration from Missouri's expansive skies, the French blue shirts, coupled with dark navy trousers, encapsulate the state's unique blend of progress and tradition.

'The ensemble is completed with a khaki campaign hat, a stylish emblem of authority that captures the essence of Missouri's proud heritage,' the ranking notes.

4. New Jersey State Police 

Drawing some similarities to Missouri, the uniforms of the New Jersey State Police showcase a French blue shirt complemented by trousers in a deep midnight blue hue. 

But this ensemble goes further by incorporating a robust belt, symbolizing New Jersey's industrial might and unyielding spirit.

The website also takes note of the badge, describing it as 'radiant with commitment, completes the outfit, echoing the state's promise of steadfast service and dedication.'

5.  Arizona Department of Public Safety

Officers in the sunbaked Southwest proudly don the fifth sexiest uniform, belonging to the Arizona Department of Public Safety. 

Their tan shirts and matching trousers stand as a testament to maintaining a sense of desert cool under the scorching sun, the website says. 

The color palette mirrors the endless sands of the region, set against the backdrop of cacti and canyons. 

The ensemble is completed with a Montana Peak hat, strategically casting just enough shadow to add an air of mystery to the wearer's gaze.

6. Colorado State Patrol

Inspired by the unique landscape of their state, the Colorado State Patrol's uniform takes its place with light blue shirts contrasting elegantly with tan trousers. 

The shirts mirror the crystal-clear alpine lakes, while the tan trousers stand as steadfast as the Rockies themselves.

The ranking notes that 'this uniform exudes a magnetic allure, embodying the state's adventurous spirit and the rugged charm of those who wear it.' 

7. Washington State Patrol

Claiming the seventh spot, the uniforms of the Washington State Patrol seamlessly merge authority with accessibility. 

Their modern cut is not just a reflection of style but also mirrors the state's forward-thinking ethos.

The belt becomes more than an accessory by symbolizing community commitment, radiating integrity and dedication.

8. Florida Highway Patrol

Florida's uniform claims the eighth spot on the list by capturing the essence of endless summer in the Sunshine State. 

The uniform achieves a delicate balance between professionalism and a pinch of holiday spirit, embodying the warmth and welcoming vibe of Florida,' according to the ranking.

9. Hawaii State Sheriff Division

The department awarded with the title of the best-dressed public safety division in 2023 also secures a well-deserved spot on the list of the sexiest uniforms. 

The Sheriff Division has boldly embraced a vibrant green, paying homage to the lush landscapes of the islands and reflecting the royal origins of the Sheriff's office under King Kamehameha III.

10. Indiana State Police

In a commanding shade of navy, the Indiana State Police's uniform claims its place among the ten sexiest police uniforms. 

It seamlessly combines tradition with modernity, symbolizing Indiana's unique blend of heartland values and contemporary design. 

The dark navy-blue uniform, cinched with a belt, not only exudes authority but also nods to the state's industrious spirit.
