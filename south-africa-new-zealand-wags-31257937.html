Meet the Rugby World Cup final WAGs cheering on South Africa and New Zealand

New Zealand and South Africa stars will be cheered on by the most glamorous of WAGs during the Rugby World Cup final at the Stade de France this Saturday

New Zealand and South Africa stars will battle it out for Rugby World Cup glory at the Stade de France - as their WAGs steal the show in the stands.

The two most dominant teams in the history of the competition with three overall wins each go head-to-head after a brutal demolition of Argentina and narrow win over England in their respective semi-finals. The showpiece event brings to an end more than seven weeks of competition which begun with a France win over the All-Blacks.

The tournament has included 320 tries for an average of just under seven per match, Will Jordan is looking to take the outright record in one competition to nine in Saint-Denis.

With no side remaining unbeaten in the competition after the Springboks lost to Ireland in Pool B, Daily Star Sport have taken a look at 12 of the partners that will be cheering on their heroes - including a New Zealand international footballer.
