Race Result | 01 May 2024 | Happy Valley | Race 9 PARISLONGCHAMP HANDICAP - Sec2 | HK Racing

Racing Incident Report

Horse No 1 COLOURFUL EMPEROR (H167) Y L Chung Sent for sampling post-race.

Horse No 2 KAHOLO ANGEL (H216) E C W Wong When questioned, Apprentice E C W Wong stated that if his mount commenced well, he was given the option to go forward.

He said when KAHOLO ANGEL jumped only fairly and did not show pace in the early stages, he elected to shift his mount behind runners to avoid covering additional ground.

Horse No 3 SOLAR PARTNER (E364) H Bentley Sent for sampling post-race.

Horse No 4 ARMOUR EAGLE (G093) Z Purton After travelling a short distance was eased when crowded between CALIFORNIA TOUCH and HAPPY TRIO which shifted in slightly.

After the race, Z Purton reported that his mount did not appreciate the "Good to Yielding" track conditions.

A veterinary inspection immediately following the race did not show any significant findings.

Horse No 7 HAPPY TRIO (G244) L Hewitson After the race, L Hewitson stated that his mount was reluctant to improve to the inside of MY FLYING ANGEL making the Home Turn.

Horse No 8 LIGHTNING BOLT (G130) K Teetan Jumped only fairly and then was crowded by ACT OF FAITH.

Horse No 9 FORTUNE WHISKEY (J153) B Thompson Jumped only fairly.

Near the 800 Metres blundered.

Horse No 10 FLOOF (H432) A Atzeni Bumped shortly after the start and became unbalanced.

From the 700 Metres was left racing wide and without cover.

A veterinary inspection immediately following the race did not show any significant findings.

Horse No 11 MY FLYING ANGEL (J174) A Badel Bumped at the start.

After the race, A Badel stated that his mount, which was having its first race start at Happy Valley from a wide barrier, did not appreciate the "Good to Yielding" track conditions.

A veterinary inspection immediately following the race did not show any significant findings.
