TV Clare Nasir drops five sizes in four months

The ex-GMTV presenter has gone from “a lime green blob” to curvy sex kitten in just four months.

Clare, 40, proudly showed off her new size-six figure after being pregnant with baby Sienna, now 13 months, made her balloon to 12st 7lb.

The new mum was a size 16 and weighed 10st 4lb when she was snapped in her bikini during a workout session.

Personal trainers Elia Siap-eras, 26, and Dee Thresher, 36, helped Clare to change her diet radically and begin a combat-style workout regime to shed the baby weight.

Clare, who is just 5ft tall, now weighs 8st.

After shifting her flab she is now sharing the secret of her success in a new DVD, Clare Nasir’s Boot Camp.

Clare, who now works for ITV London, said: “Seeing pictures of yourself looking like a lime green blob is a pretty good incentive to lose weight.

“I wanted to lose it quickly as well as safely and after four months and lots of sweat, I’ve lost an amazing 12 inches off my belly alone.

“Boxers in training burn around 8,000 calories a day and with this combat-style workout you’ll burn up to 1,000 calories every time you do it. That, combined with our amazing five-meals-a-day diet, will help you drop weight fast.

“If you follow the routine three or four times a week with the diet you could lose up to four pounds a week, which is incredible.”

Clare Nasir’s Boot Camp DVD goes on sale nationwide on Monday.
