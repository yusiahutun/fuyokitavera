Secretlab and League of Legends Join Forces With New Gaming Chairs

League of Legends fans can rejoice, as their posterior is about to become more comfortable, thanks to a crossover between Riot Games and Secretlab.

Check Out These League of Legends Gaming Chairs

Secretlab has joined up with Riot Games to release three awesome new gaming chairs, all based around characters from the game. The new Ruination-themed seats are League versions of Secretlab's recently announced Titan EVO 2022 model, so you can be sure that these gaming chairs include Secretlab's latest seating tech.

As mentioned, there are three models available. These are:

  • Secretlab Titan EVO 2022 League of Legends Pyke Edition
  • Secretlab Titan EVO 2022 League of Legends Viego Edition
  • Secretlab Titan EVO 2022 League of Legends Miss Fortune Edition

The Pyke Edition comes in black, with red accents, and the character's signature Bone Skewer harpoon sewn into the backrest.

The Viego Edition is also black, with gold and gray marble accenting. Viego's glowing Blade of the Ruined King dominates the backrest in a supernatural blue stitch.

Miss Fortune's Secretlab gaming chair comes in black, with red and white paneling and gold accents. The Gun Goddess' blunderbuss pistols appear on the backrest in gold.

The rear of each chair has a backrest emblazoned with motifs that represent each character.

All variations are available in small, regular, or extra large sizes, guaranteeing comfort for all gamers. The Secretlab x League of Legends gaming chair series is available to buy from July 23rd, just head to the Secretlab website.

What Other Features Do the League Gaming Chairs Have?

Aside from the regalia, you're probably wondering what else is special about these seats. Well, they're piled high with features, all of which have comfort in mind. So, what are we looking at?

Well, you have the memory foam seats. These are an open style seat, covered in synthetic leather, with high-density foam cushioning.

The backrests can recline up to 160 degrees, and feature adjustable lumbar support, along with lumbar cushions and a neck cushion.

The armrests are 4D. So, they can move in four directions; forwards and backwards, side to side, up and down, and they also pivot around the armrest supports.

They have five wheels, so moving around your gaming space won't be a problem.

In all, you have a luxury gaming chair that Secretlab has designed to allow for extended gaming sessions.

Anyone who is used to playing League for hours should understand the benefits of being comfortable—and in control of that comfort—while you game. Your comfort can actually be the difference between a win and a loss.

Will You Buy the Secretlab x League of Legends Gaming Chairs?

We hedge our bets and suggest that these thrones are going to be popular among the League community. They'd make an impressive addition to a League streamer's setup, at the very least!
