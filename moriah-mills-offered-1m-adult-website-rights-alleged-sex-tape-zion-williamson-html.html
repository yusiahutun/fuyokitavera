Moriah Mills offered $1M by adult website for rights to her alleged sex tape with Zion Williamson

The bidding has begun on a possible sex tape that porn star Moriah Mills may-or-may-not have of herself and New Orleans Pelicans star Zion Williamson.

A fan subscription site, Unfiltrd, is offering Mills $1million to get exclusive rights to the alleged recording.

This offer comes after the adult performer threatened to release the video once she learned that Williamson is expecting a child with his girlfriend, Ahkeema.


'Unfiltrd is increasing in popularity due to the onboarding of household names in the adult entertainment industry,' said Stephanie Matto, founder and CEO of Unfiltrd. 

'We are confident that Mills will be able to monetize her explicit content on Unfiltrd in ways that she won't be able to elsewhere.'

Mills has previously posted on Twitter - threatening to release the alleged tapes publicly 

Mills has posted many times to try and get Williamson's attention. This has included a video of herself getting a 'tattoo' of Zion Williamson's name on her face and a positive pregnancy test.

This proposition includes the rights to videos and direct messages between her and Williamson. It also includes a video release event taking place at a nightclub in New Orleans.

Mills' management was contacted last Sunday with a one-week deadline to respond - meaning she must do so by July 2. It's not clear if Mills accepted or declined the offer.

The adult performer has spent the past few weeks publicly attacking the forward on social media after Williamson posted behind-the-scenes images of a gender reveal party for his soon-to-arrive child.

Mills has been trying to get attention from Williamson - to which he has not publicly made a comment.

Her attempts have including posting a picture of a positive pregnancy test on her Instagram story. She also posted a poll asking followers what she should name her child - with the options being 'Junior' and 'Zoriah', a blend of Zion and Moriah.

Williamson and his girlfriend shared a behind the scenes look at their gender reveal June 6

All this seems to suggest that the child is Williamson's, however there is no evidence publicly available to determine whether or not this is the case.

Mills also posted a video of herself getting a 'tattoo' of Zion's name on her face. 

However, there are a number of indicators that this was faked - including a full face of makeup she had on the area she was getting tattooed as well as a lack of redness and swelling around the area.

Additionally, in subsequent posts to social media, Mills has avoided showing the side of her face that the tattoo was allegedly on.

She has also gone on lengthy tirades of multiple posts on Twitter and Instagram - which led to both accounts being temporarily suspended.

Mills has also previously uploaded multiple screenshots of alleged conversations she and Williamson sent - including messages discussing Zion sending her money and offering to book a flight for her.

Williamson has also been described as a 'nasty sex addict' by Yamille Taylor, a 'digital creator'

In the caption on the posts containing those the screenshots, Mills wrote: 'Where is my wire transfer Zion Williamson, you a liar and a cheat where the f*** is my money,' before including the Pelicans and urging them to 'trade this fraud'. 

In many other tweets, Mills claimed 'it's over for you' and insisted his 'NBA career light will be dimmed once the sex tapes release.'

Williamson has still yet to make comments of any kind about this situation. But others have made similar accusations as Mills.

Weeks ago, a third woman - Yamille Taylor - blasted the former Duke player, calling him a 'sex addict'.

While all this drama was going on, Williamson donated $250k through his foundation to students in the Jefferson Parish School district on the outskirts of New Orleans, according to ESPN.
