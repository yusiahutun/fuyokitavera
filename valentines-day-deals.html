40 Best Valentine's Day Food Deals 2024

Valentine's Day is the perfect time to show the love, but between the Valentine's Day gifts, thoughtful bouquets of fresh roses, and special date nights, February 14 can get expensive. The average person spends almost $200 on the day, so it's understandable if you are looking to save a few bucks. One place you can find some good deals is on those Valentine's Day dinners. Many restaurants have specials, so check out this list before you make your plans. Whether you’re in the market for a spread for the whole family or a romantic dinner for two, ahead you’ll find 40 Valentine’s Day restaurant deals to consider. Best of all, many of them start before and end after the day of love, so you can take advantage of them beyond Monday, February 14. Don't feel like leaving the house? Order through Uber Eats or Postmates.

Baskin-Robbins

Nothing says "love" like ice cream! Baskin-Robbins locations are offering their Love Potion #31 flavor that combines white chocolate and raspberry flavored ice creams, raspberry-filled chocolate flavored hearts, raspberry swirls, and chocolate flavored chips. Or go for the heart-shaped "Crazy for You" cake (use code LOVE for $3 off).

Bertucci's

Bertucci's has 31 locations from New England to Virginia. This year, the Italian restaurant offered a three-course dinner for two with a choice of salad, entrée (including spaghetti and meatballs, chicken parmesan, and shrimp Rossini), plus dessert to share for $39.99. If you're feeding the family, add a kid's meal for $5 or a heart-shaped pizza for $9.99.

Bonefish Grill

If you want to celebrate early, make reservations for the Prisoner of Love wine pairing dinner on February 7. The four-course meal costs $65. Or treat your Valentine to one of Bonefish Grill's seasonal specials, like Smoky Tomato Butter Grouper or Old Fashioned Cedar Plank Salmon. Top it off with a decadent chocolate lava cake.

California Pizza Kitchen

CPK is making it easy to celebrate at home or in-restaurant with its Valentine’s Day Sweet Deal for Two. The deal runs from February 9 to 14 and includes an appetizer, two entrees, and a dessert. Guests can choose between the Classic option (which includes dishes like white corn guacamole and chips, garlic cream fettuccine with chicken, and red velvet cake) for $50 or the Adventure option (with items like dynamite shrimp, cedar plank salmon, and Belgian chocolate souffle cake) for $60. Each option offers pizzas, too, which come in an adorable heart shape. If you're dining in, you'll get a bonus card to use on your next visit.

CPK

Carrabba’s Italian Grill

A delicious 4-course dinner for two starting at just $65? You can thank Carrabba’s for that. The deal, which is available from February 8 through 18, includes an appetizer, two salads or soups, two entrees, and one dessert. There are over a dozen different dishes to choose from, including tomato caprese with burrata, caesar salad, chicken marsala, Tuscan-grilled sirloin, and strawberry cheesecake. Select locations start with a celebratory glass of Prosecco.

Chevys Fresh Mex

The special three-course Valentine's Day menu for two ($55) at Chevys Fresh Mex includes tortilla soup or a house salad, an entrée of chicken or steak fajitas, mix-and-match fajitas, shrimp-and-crab enchiladas, or the surf n’ turf combo. Save room for a shared dessert of butter cake, a brownie sundae, deep fried ice cream, or cinnamon-crisp cheesecake.

Chick-Fil-A

Chick-fil-A

Chick-Fil-A is pulling out the stops again for Valentine's Day with their heart-shaped trays filled with 30-count Chick-fil-A Nuggets, 10-count Chick-n-Minis (during breakfast hours only), 12 Chocolate Fudge Brownie halves, or six Chocolate Chunk Cookies. Have a fun Valentine's Day breakfast, or pick up a tray to leave with the babysitter.

Cooper's Hawk Winery & Restaurants

Cooper's Hawk has more than 60 locations nationwide. If there's one near you, take advantage of the Cooper's Hawk Surf & Turf or Blue Crab Stuffed Lobster Tails (available February 8-18).

Cracker Barrel

Want to treat that special someone to something golden this year? Try the new Golden Carolina BBQ Tenders featuring Cracker Barrel's bold and tangy Carolina BBQ sauce. They won't be available to dine-in customers until February 27, but you can order them now for carryout or through Door Dash with a Dash Pass membership. Of course, Cracker Barrel stores offer plenty of gift ideas in their shops.

Dairy Queen

Don't pass up the opportunity for a sweet treat! Dairy Queen is offering two special themed desserts: the Red Velvet Cake Blizzard Treat and Red Velvet Cake Blizzard Cupid Cake for two.

Donatos Pizza

This year, pick up heart-shaped pizzas from Donatos (February 10-February 18). The one-topping pizzas cost the same as a medium pizza and is served uncut.

El Torito

With locations across California, El Torito is offering a three-course menu for two ($55). Choose from tortilla soup or a Caesar salad, then choose from entrées including fajitas, salmon, or enchiladas, and a shared dessert.

Fazoli's

Purchase any entree at Fazoli's and get a free Cheesecake Factory Bakery Cheesecake or Italian Cream Cake. Use code VALENTINES24 at participating locations to get the deal.

Firehouse Subs

Keep it casual this year and pick up sandwiches from Firehouse Subs. This year, participating restaurants are offering a free medium sub with purchase of an additional medium or large sub, chips, and a drink (after 4 p.m. on February 14). Show the post on their Instagram page to redeem the deal.

Fogo De Chão

For a special Valentine's Day meal, make reservations at Fogo De Chão. Their Churrasco Experience offers a full array of meats, salads, sides, and desserts, plus an impressive wine and cocktail list.

Giordano’s

Nothing says Valentine's Day like a heart-shaped Chicago-style pizza. Order at a local Giordano's restaurant from February 5-14 (prices vary based on toppings), or have a two-, four-, or six-pack delivered anywhere across the country through the website.

Hungry Howie's

The nationwide chain is offering heart-shaped pizzas and heart-shaped 3-Cheeser Howie Bread for two days only: February 13 and 14. The pizzas are available with pepperoni or just cheese, and the Howie Bread is served with pizza sauce. Both cost $7.99.

IHOP

Starting on February 1, IHOP locations are offering a special pancake of the month. Just in time for Valentine's Day, treat your sweetie to Chocolate Strawberry Pancakes: Four chocolate chocolate chip pancakes topped with cheesecake mousse, then drizzled with chocolate sauce, sliced strawberries, whipped topping, and chocolate chips.

Hard Rock Cafe

Hard Rock

Toast your love with a special Flirtini cocktail, a mix of vodka with citrus, pineapple, and Chambord topped with Prosecco Rosé (available all month). For the main course at Hard Rock Cafe, try the Surf and Turf dinner of strip steak scampi with a complimentary side of onion rings (available February 6-16).

KFC

Try the new Smash’d Potato Bowls at KFC. The comforting meal features mashed potatoes topped with fries, warm cheese sauce, bacon crumbles, and three-cheese blend. The bowls cost $3.49 each, but you and your significant other can take advantage of the BOGO deal starting Feb. 5 (order online or through the app). If you're looking for something more traditional, try the new $20 Taste of KFC Meal with six pieces of Original Recipe chicken plus four and four biscuits.

Krispy Kreme

The Krispy Kreme Valentine's Day dozen is the perfect treat for anyone special. This year, they're offering four new heart-shaped doughnuts (You Color My World Doughnut, I Love You A Choco-Lot Doughnut, You’re Berry Sweet Doughnut, and Without You I’d Crumble Doughnut). Available now through February 14.

Logan’s Roadhouse

Guests can enjoy a delicious Valentine’s Day dinner in-restaurant, for carryout, or delivery at Logan’s Roadhouse. From February 14-18, Logan’s is offering a holiday-specific menu, which is particularly great for to-go orders. It includes two 12-ounce prime rib dinners with a choice of chocolate cake or cheesecake.

Marcos Pizza

The heart-shaped pizza costs just $9.99 at Marco's Pizza. Check the website for the promo code.

Melting Pot

The Melting Pot

For a memorable romantic Valentine’s Day dinner any day in February, book a table at the Melting Pot. Prefer to celebrate without leaving the house? Try the Melting Pot at Home offerings, which feature signature cheese fondue paired with Omaha Steaks dippers.

Metro Diner

Kick off Valentine's Day on a sweet note with a special breakfast at Metro Diner. Go for Strawberry Shortcake Griddles featuring bread pudding, Belgian waffle, or pancakes topped with strawberry cream cheese icing and fresh strawberries, and a cobbler crumble. Toast the day with a Pretty in Pink Mimosa.

Moe's Southwest Grill

Trust us, your Valentine wants queso. If you spend $30 in Moe's gift cards online, you'll receive $10 Moe Rewards (valid through February 15). Note: You must have the Moe's app, and the reward must be redeemed by 4/30.

Morton’s

From February 9 to 17, Morton’s is encouraging a dine-in date with its $169 3-course menu for two. The dinner includes a choice of soup or salad, a 28-ounce 'Empire Cut' Bone-In New York Strip, Twin Cold Water Lobster Tails and sides.

Outback Steakhouse

Head to Outback and treat your date to a special meal. This year, Outback is offering a 4-course meal for two including grilled shrimp or cheese fries, two salads, two entrées (sirloin, salmon, or chicken), and cheesecake starting at $60. No matter what you choose, be sure to toast the day with the Berry Passiontini cocktail.

Papa John’s

Courtesy of Papa John's

Hosting a cozy Valentine’s Day night in? Papa John’s is once again offering its famous heart-shaped pizza for carryout and delivery from February 5 to 14. A one-topping thin crust is just $11.99.

Perry’s Steakhouse and Grille

Perry’s, which has 21 locations across seven states, is offering a three-course prix fixe menu including a choice of soup or salad, an entrée, and a dessert. Guests can choose between dishes like lobster bisque, an eight-ounce filet, white chocolate cheesecake, and more. The price varies based on the time of your reservation, starting at $69 per person for dining before 5:30 p.m. and going up to $79 per person for reservations at 5:45 or later. The restaurant is also offering a special candlelight dinner for two from February 11-14 ($185 per couple). Both options are available to-go too.

Pizza Hut

Prefer Pizza Hut’s crust above all? This year, try the new Hot Honey Double Pepperoni Pizza for $12.99. Select locations will be offering heart-shaped pizzas this year, but you'll want to confirm in advance.

Pizza Hut

Playa Bowl

On February 2, Playa Bowl is debuting the new Bae Bowl, featuring half Playa Pitaya Base and half Stupid Cupid Base topped with blueberry flax granola, strawberries, coconut flakes, goji berries, and a pitaya infused white chocolate drizzle. Playa Rewards members can take advantage of a BOGO deal on the Bae Bowl on February 14 (in store only).

Popeyes

The limited-edition Heart-Shaped Strawberry Biscuits are in Popeyes restaurants for three weeks only (February 5-25). They're only $1.79 each, and the chicken chain is donating a portion of the proceeds to their Popeyes Foundation’s SERVING WITH LOVE® initiative.

Potbelly

Potbelly perks members will receive a complimentary cookie with the purchase of an entrée. it's the perfect time to try the new red velvet cookie.

QDOBA

On February 14, QDOBA is offering rewards members a free entrée when they buy an entrée and a drink. Treat your significant other, or save the second meal for lunch tomorrow!

Red Lobster

The seafood restaurant's famed Lobsterfest is back just in time for Valentine's Day. Try the new offerings of Lobster & Shrimp Lover's Dream, Crispy Lobster & Shrimp Stack, or Lobster Flatbread.

Round Table Pizza

Take $5 off a large heart-shaped pizza or a regular XL pizza from Round Table Pizza. Use code HEART5 at checkout.

Ruth’s Chris Steak House

Whether you’re looking to celebrate your date night in the restaurant or comfortably at home, Ruth’s Chris is here to help. The famous steak house is offering a Porterhouse Dinner for Two (February 14 only) or signature T-Bone with Horseradish Crust (February 9-18). Go ahead and make a reservation now. The restaurant offers seating as early as 4 p.m. but they fill up quickly.

Seasons 52 Grill and Wine Bar

Seasons 52 is offering a take-out option for diners who wish to celebrate at home. The Valentine’s Day Dinner for Two includes two 6-ounce filets with sides, salads, and two desserts for $80 (available February 10-18).

Schlotzsky’s

Buy a $30 gift card at Schlotzsky’s and get a free cinnamon roll from Cinnabon.

Shake Shack

Shake Shack fans can order a Korean BBQ Burger or Korean Style Fried Chicken Sandwich, and get a second for free. The BOGO deal is available nationwide when ordering delivery on Shake Shack’s website or the Shack App.

Starbucks

Starbucks

This year, go for the new Chocolate-Covered Strawberry Crème Frappuccino at Starbucks.. It blends ice, milk, strawberry puree, and java chips, layered with strawberry puree and topped with whipped cream. It pairs the classic Valentine's Day combination of strawberries and chocolate.

TGI Fridays

Head to the popular chain for a three-course feast for two. This year, TGI Fridays is offering a three-course meal for two starting at $20 per person. The offer is valid through 2/16 for dine-in service only.
