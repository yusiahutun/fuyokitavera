Kendall Jenner Does Winter Maximalism in a Hand-Painted Fur Coat

In a new slideshow Kendall Jenner posted to Instagram, the 28-year-old model looks like she’s holding court on an episode of Game of Thrones—and we’d be posing the same way if we had a jacket this impressive.

The Kardashians star attended an event over the weekend co-hosted by FWRD (of which she is the creative director) and her own tequila company, 818. For the occasion, Jenner wore a giant chocolate-brown fur coat from Phoebe Philo, hand-painted with black fur around the sleeves and featuring an equally furry black belt. The shaggy outerwear was layered over sheer black tights, and Jenner rounded out the look with pretty black ballet flats and drop earrings composed of tiny gold roses.

“Turning into an (818) espresso martini,” Jenner wrote in her caption. Fittingly, the model included a photo of herself holding that specific cocktail in one of the slides, where she also showed off her finely manicured French tips, adding a dainty feminine touch to her otherwise bold, maximalist look.

Jenner has been embracing all kinds of chocolatey hues as of late. A couple of weeks back, the supermodel documented a dinner on Instagram, at which she wore a vintage Alaïa leather brown jacket and matching skirt suit while posing with a glass of wine.

She has also worn a streak of stunning coats lately. On Monday night, Jenner was spotted during a getaway to Aspen wearing a white woolly coat over a black turtleneck and slacks.
