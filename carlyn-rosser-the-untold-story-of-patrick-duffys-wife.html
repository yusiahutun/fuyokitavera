Carlyn Rosser: The Untold Story of Patrick Duffy's Wife

Here’s everything we know about Carlyn Rosser.

Who Is Carlyn Rosser?

According to Duffy, his late wife was so passionate about art that she bought an art piece with her entire weekly salary, which eventually became the first art piece he ever held. How Did Patrick Duffy and Carlyn Rosser Meet?

Rosser met the man who would later become her husband on a tour bus. She was already an accomplished ballet dancer. However, Duffy had just graduated from college and was touring as a narrator with a dance production company. According to the renowned actor, it was love at first sight, and he instantly knew she was the one.

When they met, Carlyn Rosser was a married woman ten years older than Duffy. However, despite her age difference and marital status, the actor revealed that he fell in love with her art. Since Rosser and her then-husband, Dale Roy Muchmore, had been together for over 13 years, telling him she had met someone else proved difficult.

Eventually, Carlyn Rosser told her then-husband about her newfound lover, and despite feeling betrayed, he let her go. Six months after Carlyn Rosser’s death, her husband took to Twitter to pay her a sweet tribute in a Tweet. While Patrick Duffy’s wife, Carlyn Rosser’s cause of death is unclear, reports suggest that the “Dallas” star’s wife lost her battle with cancer. However, these reports remain unconfirmed. Following Rosser’s death, the actor spent most of his time as a widower, raising their sons until he was ready to move on.
