Flip Wilson – Biography

Flip Wilson was born in Jersey City, NJ on December 8, 1933. Two-time Emmy Award-winning African American comedian who often played the sassy Geraldine on the television variety show, The Flip Wilson Show.

He was known to have influenced the comedic style of Chris Rock. He lied about his age and joined the U.S. Air Force at 16 years old after bouncing around foster homes.
