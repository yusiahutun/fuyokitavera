Kim Zolciak's estranged husband Kroy Biermann has been offered $150,000 by a Las Vegas strip club Kings of Hustler to 'perform a live strip tease' amid his ongoing financial troubles.

Biermann, 38, was offered the six-figure sum after he submit court docs earlier this week claiming both he and Real Housewives Of Atlanta star, 45, were 'financially destitute' as result of her 'reckless spending habits and love for online gambling.'

The club told TMZ that for the $150,000 fee, Biermann would have to dance in his underwear for at least 15 minutes, and give lap dances 'at the request of big spenders.'

He would have to ride on a phallic-shaped mechanical bull at the club that is  nicknamed the 'Golden Boner.'

Kings of Hustler manager Brittany Rose told the outlet that Biermann would likely 'make a ton of money in tips' in addition to his base pay.
