Sebastian Taylor Thomaz Age, Parents, Siblings, Net Worth &amp; Family

Khalifa’s fans are curious to know more details about his son, Thomaz. Currently, Thomaz is just 6 years old and he is too young to get married or date any girl.

Who Is Sebastian Taylor Thomaz? Bio & Wiki

Sebastian Taylor Thomaz was born on 21st February 2013, Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, United States. He is an American by his nationality and belongs to African-American ethnicity. He is the son of Amber Rose (mother), a model and Wiz Khalifa (father).

Thomaz is the granddaughter of Michael Levonchuck, Dorothy Rose (from his mother’s side), and Peachie Wimbush, Laurence Thomaz (from his father’s side). He is also the nephew of Dorien Lala Thomaz and Antonio Hewlett.

Sebastian Taylor Thomaz hasn’t pursued his career, but his father got his first breakthrough when Rostrum Records president, Benjy Grinberg heard his voice in 2004. Later, Grinberg decided to work with him, and Thomaz released his first mixtape, Prince of the City: Welcome to Pistolvania in 2005.

Thomaz released his debut album, Show and Proved in 2006. Furthermore, he recorded his third album, Deal or No Deal in 2009. He also released and composed his fourth album, Rolling Papers in 2011. Similarly, Khalifa released many studio albums, including Mac & Devin Go to High School (featuring Snoop Dog), Blacc Hollywood, and others.
