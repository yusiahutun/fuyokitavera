Made In America Announces Lineup

PHILADELPHIA, PA (CelebrityAccess) — The Jay-Z curated two-day Made In America music festival will return to Philadelphia for its eighth year in 2019.

Set for Labor Day Weekend, the Roc Nation-produced festival features a lineup that includes Lil Uzi Vert, Lizzo, Gucci Mane, Dominic Fike, Alina Baraz, Jay Critch, Melii, Buddy, IDK, Charly Bliss, and Angelica Vila.

That’s in addition to previously the list of previously announced performers that includes Travis Scott, Cardi B, Juice WRLD, James Blake, Kaskade, Anderson .Paak & The Free Nationals, Kodak Black, Bazzi, Jorja Smith, Blueface, Pink Sweat$, Jacob Banks, KAYTRANADA, and Grace Carter among others.

The festival will take place at Benjamin Franklin Parkway and will also be live-streamed via the TIDAL streaming music platform.

As with previous years, a portion of the festival’s net proceeds will go to benefit the ACLU of Pennsylvania, who has been named as the fest’s official charity partner.

A portion of net proceeds will also support The REFORM Alliance, which advocates for criminal justice reform.
