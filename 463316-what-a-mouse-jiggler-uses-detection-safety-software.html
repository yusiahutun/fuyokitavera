What is a Mouse Jiggler?

Mouse Jiggler is a simple tool with which you can simulate the movement of your mouse. This allows you not to see your screen saver, and your system is not put into hibernation. It enables you to keep your computer awake without touching a mouse.

What is the point of a Mouse Mover?

It is a motion simulator device for a computer mouse. The device works by nudging the computer mouse cursor into action. As a result, it fools the computer into thinking it's being used. Then, it prevents the sleep mode or the computer screensaver from activating.

Is Mouse Jiggler safe?

The Mouse Jiggler is undoubtedly safe. However, you may run into problems depending on the Mouse Mover you get. For example, using a USB jiggler will trick the computer into thinking that the jiggler is your mouse, preventing you from using your main mouse.

Some Mouse Jigglers can also be detected by your antivirus software as unsafe. Therefore, your computer may not allow them to function in the first place.
