I'm A Celeb viewers blown away as Nadine Coyle wows in bikini turned see-through

Nadine Coyle turned up the heat on tonight's I'm a Celebrity... Get Me Out of Here!.

The Girls Aloud star, 34, sent hearts racing, as she rocked a seriously skimpy bikini in the jungle shower.

Nadine wowed as she donned a teeny white two-piece, which accentuated her jungle-earned tan.

Nadine enjoyed the jungle shower with her equally-glamorous campmates Kate Garraway, 52, and Caitlyn Jenner, 70.

Good Morning Britain host Kate looked sensational in a plunging red bikini, while Caitlyn wowed in a floral one-piece.

Viewers were left floored by Nadine's shower display.

Taking to Twitter to share their delight, one social networker enthused: "Nadine shower scene... FINALLY."

While another continued: "Finally got to see Nadine in the shower."

Nadine has become a fan favourite on the show thanks to her down-to-earth personality and openness about her Girls Aloud past.

In a chat with EastEnders star Jacqueline Jossa, Nadine addressed her rumoured long-standing feud with former bandmate Cheryl Tweedy.

Jacqueline revealed rumours of a feud were "fake news".

Jacqueline asked Nadine, 34, what it was like being in a girlband.

She said: "Really good, really good, I like being in a girl band. I am one of 3 sisters and went to an all-girls grammar school so I’m used to being around girls."

Jacqueline asked if Girls Aloud were put together like Little Mix.

Nadine replied: "Really good, really good, I like being in a girl band. I am one of 3 sisters and went to an all-girls grammar school so I’m used to being around girls."

I'm a Celebrity... Get Me Out of Here! continues tomorrow at 9pm on ITV
