Insomniac To Bring Beyond Wonderland To Bogota

BOGATA, Colombia (CelebrityAccess) — EDM festival producer Insomniac announced its expanding its Beyond Wonderland festival brand to the Colombian capital of Bogata.

The festival, which will be produced by Insomniac and OCESA will take place on Nov. 9th at Parque El Salitre, a centrally located park in downtown Bogata that includes both an amusement park and waterpark.

While other details about the festival, including the lineup, have not yet been announced, tickets went on sale on July 2nd.

Insomniac is no stranger to producing events in Central and South America and since 2014, have staged festivals such as EDC Mexico, Factory 93, Dreamstate, and Beyond Wonderland to markets in the region.

“Very excited to announce that Beyond will be making its debut in South America this year! Beyond Wonderland comes to Bogotá, Colombia on November 9, and we’ve got a beautiful day of art and music planned for you,” Insomniac’s Pasquale Rotella said in a social media post announcing the festival.

“I’ll be racking up some solid frequent flyer miles that weekend going to both EDC Orlando and South America, but I wouldn’t want to miss the chance to vibe with all our new Headliners and experience Beyond in Bogotá! See you in November!” he added.
