My Hair Grew 2 Inches in Just 2 Months Thanks to This Thickening Oil

About a year ago, I made the decision to cut my hair. This was a big deal, considering I was attached to my long, glamorous locks. Though once I made the chop, I was addicted; I went shorter every other month, and my once free-flowing mane barely reached chin level (think, Hailey Bieber chic). But after 10 months, I wanted my long tresses back, and thus started my hair growth journey. I tried just about everything including collagen gummies, hair growth shampoos, serums, and more, but nothing quite worked like the Kreyòl Essence Haitian Black Castor Oil.     

The Kreyòl Essence Haitian Black Castor Oil comes from an eco-friendly brand that supplies economic opportunities for the women of Haiti. Dubbed “liquid gold” by the brand during a segment of Shark Tank, the nourishing $28 oil can be used on the scalp, eyelashes, and eyebrows. It includes ricinoleic acid, which is the “fatty acid component” that “increase[s] blood flow to the hair follicles,” Dr. DiAnne Davis, a board-certified cosmetic dermatologist, previously told InStyle. Coupled with omega fats, the concoction improves hair health by moisturizing the scalp and creating an environment for restoration.  

Kreyòl Essence Haitian Black Castor Oil

I mix five dropperfuls of the castor oil with three dropperfuls of rosemary oil and apply the mixture to my scalp three times a week. I work the product in utilizing a scalp massager, like this on-sale $8 Heeta find, wrap my hair in an overnight sleep cap, and go to bed. I wash the product out in the morning using a detoxifying scrub or with a double rinse. But I get it, sometimes you don’t have all the time in the world. Leaving the product in for 30 minutes before a shower also gets the job done. Another hack? Use the castor oil as a styling aid like Bieber. You’ll achieve a balletcore bun with hair health benefits.   

It’s been two months since I started using the Kreyòl Essence castor oil, and my hair has grown two inches. Being that my hair usually takes months to grow an inch, this is a major feat. It’s also helped with frizziness, shine, and strength, and while I still have a ways to go when it comes to my hair goals, I may be giving Rapunzel a run for her money. 

Shoppers are blown away by the results too, with one saying their “hair is growing like wildfire.” “I had postpartum shedding on my edges and they were practically bald,” said another reviewer. After “two weeks,” they noticed new hair growth and would now “recommend [the oil] to anyone!” Even more impressive, a shopper noticed hair growth after just one week, claiming her “hair was growing like crazy!” This isn’t just for the ladies, either. One male user saw results after two weeks, and experienced tremendous hair growth after three months. In short? It’s a “great product and worth the money,” according to the same buyer.    

The Kreyòl Essence Haitian Black Castor Oil is also proven to help with various skin conditions. “I have horrible eczema on my foot,” said an Amazon shopper. “I have only been using this item for three days and what a world of difference.” Another reviewer who has tried “over 50 products” to help their psoriasis claimed the Kreyòl castor oil has made a “huge difference” on their hands, without stinging. “This is my favorite by far,” they concluded.  

If you’re at your wits end when it comes to hair growth (or tricky skin conditions), don’t give up just yet. Give the Kreyòl Essence Haitian Black Castor Oil a shot. After all, there’s an entire roster of praise reports, including yours truly.
