About

Mel Gibson is a 68 year old American Actor. Born Mel Columcille Gerard Gibson on 3rd January, 1956 in Peekskill, New York, USA and educated at Asquith Boys High School, New South Wales, Australia, he is famous for his roles as Max “Mad Max” Rockatansky in the Mad Max film series, Martin Riggs in the Lethal Weapon film series, Sir William Wallace in the 1995 historical drama film Braveheart, and Benjamin Martin in the 2000 epic historical war drama film The Patriot. His zodiac sign is Capricorn.

Goldie Hawn is a 78 year old American Actress. Born Goldie Jeanne Hawn on 21st November, 1945 in Washington, District of Columbia, USA, she is famous for Laugh-In, Private Benjamin, Protocol. in a career that spans 1967–present and 1967–2002; 2013 (acting). Her zodiac sign is Scorpio.
