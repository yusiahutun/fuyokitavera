Gugu Mbatha-Raw and Kevin Hart were looking their best on Monday evening at the premiere of their upcoming Netflix film Lift in New York City.

The 40-year-old actress looked effortlessly elegant in a pale gray sleeveless dress, while the 44-year-old comedian hit the red carpet in a stylish double-breasted suit as he arrived at Jazz at Lincoln Center in Manhattan.

The stars lead the heist comedy, which premieres on Netflix on January 12, as part of a crew of professional thieves who taken on a seemingly impossible mission: to steal $500 million in gold bars from the cargo hold of an airplane — while it's in the air.
