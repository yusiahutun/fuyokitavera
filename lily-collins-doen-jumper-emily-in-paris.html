Lily Collins channels her Emily in Paris character in dopamine inducing jumper

Life imitates art

    It seems as though Lily Collins may have picked up a few style tips from Emily Copper, the titular character of her Netflix hit series .

    In a series of photos shared with her 27.7 million Instagram followers The 33-year old actress showed off her rather festive jumper which proves that a classic Christmas knit can continue to work its magic in January. For the rest of the outfit Lily opted for a burgundy red lipstick, light blue jeans which completed the look.

    The daughter of music legend Phil Collins donned cult brand Dôen’s ‘Brynn Sweater.’ Crafted from a recycled cashmere-wool blend and sporting  a Fair Isle-inspired design, the Brynn Sweater is “a classic pullover crewneck destined to become a cold-weather staple,” Dôen explain, “Crochet trim frames the high neck, cuffs, and hem, while subtle shirring at the shoulders lends a hint of volume to the full-length sleeves.” The style is a touch more colourful than we’ve seen Lily wearing in the past, so perhaps the avant garde wardrobe of her famous character (styled to aplomb by Sex And The City’s Patricia Field) has rubbed off on her. 

    Founded in 2016 by Santa Barbara-born sisters, Margaret and Katherine Kleveland, Dôen is a women-run business offering collections inspired by “a nostalgia for the coastal California of decades past,” the brand explains on its website. The collections themselves are a “celebration of womanhood, designed with a keen reverence for the landscapes of their youth - grazing oak groves and orchards and looking out on serene, soft hues of the oceans. Meticulously crafted to pass on for generations, the garments evoke a timeless whimsy and an unapologetic femininity.”

    Lily opted for cult-favourite brand Dôen

    The outfit paired perfectly with her new blunt fringe, which was cut for the series. Lily shared the exact moment her hair transformation was made, and as you might have guessed it wasn't the actress doing the heavy lifting, but instead A lister adored hairstylist Gregory Russell.  

    The fringe of course, suits Lily perfectly, adding a certain je ne sais quoi to her spectacular wardrobe.
