Halle Berry's ex-husband Eric Benet seems to have moved on without a hitch.

The singer not only married Prince's ex-wife, Manuela Testolini, but he's also been happily adding to his brood.

On Wednesday the 47-year-old hit maker announced to UsWeekly that he and his spouse have welcomed their second daughter, who they named Amoura Luna. He already has a 23-year-old daughter India from a previous relationship and another girl, Lucia Bella, aged two, with Manuela.

It's another girl: Eric Benet and Manuela Testolini, pictured in 2013, welcomed their second daughter, Amoura Luna, on July 13, UsWeekly announced on Wednesday

The child was born on July 13 with a 'full head of hair,' his rep explained. She weighed in at six pounds, 13 ounces.

The couple gave her the middle name Luna because she was born the night of a supermoon.

On Wednesday Benet tweeted: 'Amoura Luna officially begins her journey of growth, gratitude, abundance, and love. And she has a huge amaZing family complete with two big sisters Lucia Bella and @indiabenet to show her how it's done!! #blessed #overjoyed.'

Back in their married days: Eric with Halle - both 47-years-old now - in January 2003

The R&B artist was married to Halle from 2001 to 2005. They never had any children together.

Halle had her first child, daughter Nahla Ariela, with fashion model Gabriel Aubry in 2008. She has since had a son, Maceo Robert, who is nearly aged one, with actor husband Olivier Martinez.

Manuela married Eric in 2011. She was married to singer Prince for five years; in 2006 she filed for divorce.

Her new man: The Extant star with husband Olivier Martinez in June at a CBS event promoting her new TV series

Eric has received four Grammy nominations in his career.

His hit songs include Sometimes I Cry, Spend My Life With You and I Wanna Be Loved.

The Wisconsin native acted in the TV movie Trinity Goodheart and appeared in the series Real Husbands Of Hollywood in 2013.
