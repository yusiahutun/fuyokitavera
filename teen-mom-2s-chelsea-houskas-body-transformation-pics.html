Teen Mom 2's Chelsea Houska's Body Transformation: Pics

Hot mama! Teen Mom 2 alum Chelsea Houska has remained committed to her workout routine and healthy eating habits after welcoming four children over the years.

The HGTV star — Houska and husband Cole DeBoer’s home renovation show, Down Home Fab, premiered on the network in January 2023 — gave birth to her eldest daughter, Aubree, in September 2009. The milestone was captured on the South Dakota native’s 16 and Pregnant episode in 2010. Houska was 18 years old when she welcomed Aubree, whom she shares with ex-boyfriend Adam Lind. The Aubree Says founder went on to give birth to son Watson in 2017, followed by daughter Layne one year later and daughter Walker in 2021. Houska shares her youngest three children with DeBoer, whom she married in 2016.

Throughout 10 seasons of Teen Mom 2, viewers watched the Lauriebelles designer transform from a teenager to a mom of four. Not only did Houska hit milestone after milestone during her time on the MTV hit — getting her GED, becoming an esthetician, marrying DeBoer and more — she also kept fans in the loop about her weight loss.

In 2013, Houska posted a rare snap of herself in a bikini via Instagram, showing off her tanned and toned figure. Two years later, the 16 and Pregnant alum put her fitness progress on display in May 2015 shot in a two-piece. “Decided to add a little more cardio to my routine,” Houska explained of her workout regimen via Instagram in March of that year. “3 days crossfit 2 days kickboxing.”

The former MTV personality was also given the go-ahead from her doctor to hit the gym while pregnant with Watson in 2016. “Takin it super easy at the gym … but still wanna be a fit mama! 🏋🏻,” she wrote via Instagram at the time, alongside a photo of her at the athletic club. “Glad my dr gave the OK!”

In addition to her workout sessions, Houska made sure to “keep it real real” when showing off her postpartum body in 2017 after Watson’s birth.

“Five days postpartum,” the reality star captioned a January Instagram selfie. “Still rocking a tummy that looks a bit pregnant and some huge milk boobs. … Also, let’s keep it real real. The belly’s still chilling.”
