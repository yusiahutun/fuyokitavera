Joe Jonas spotted on 'romantic trip' with Stormi Bree but fans think Sophie's 'on his mind'

Joe Jonas and model Stormi Bree sparked romance rumors when they were spotted together at Cabo airport, and now, they’ve added more fuel to the fire as they’ve been spotted on a ‘romantic’ getaway in Aspen. However, some eagle-eyed fans have spotted a hint that ‘links to’ Sophie Turner.

The rumored celebrity couple made headlines earlier in the week when they were spotted leaving the airport together, four months after Joe Jonas announced his shock divorce from the Game of Thrones star. Sophie had already sparked rumors of a new romance two months after the divorce, with British aristocrat Peregrine Pearson.

Joe Jonas and Stormi Bree fuel romance rumors in Aspen

Joe Jonas and Stormi Bree looked pretty cozy and ‘loved up’ as they took to the slopes in Colorado, as they could be seen sharing a laugh riding the chair-lift together.

Both parties have been posting updates via their Instagram stories in the same location, although as of yet, there’s no hard launch in sight.

However, of course, eagle-eyed fans did some digging and noticed the model was wearing the Jo Bro’s wooly beanie hat on her story. The same one was worn by the music star less than a month ago, during a night out in London.

It looks like the two are trying to keep the ‘relationship’ on the down low though, as an insider at the resort told Page Six: “Joe was super smiley and [him and Bree] were just chatting and sitting together and seemed cozy,” adding there was ‘no PDA’.

“It was nice no one was bothering him,” the insider continued.

Fans spot ‘similarity’ between Joe and Sophie’s ‘new relationships’

It looks like Joe Jonas and Sophie Turner have both well and truly moved on from each other, however, some eagle-eyed fans still spotted a link between them.

The actress has been linked to British aristocrat, Peregrine Pearson since they were spotted locking lips in November.

Last month, the ‘new couple’ was spotted walking around the streets of London, where once again, they engaged in some PDA. At the time, Turner was wearing a baseball cap that read “Aspen,” and fans on X, formerly known as Twitter, are linking Joe’s trip to these pics.

“Isn’t it weird that Joe Jonas took his new girlfriend to Aspen days after Sophie Turner was spotted with her new boyfriend wearing a baseball cap that read Aspen?” questioned one fan.

In November, it was reported the music star thought it was ‘too soon’ for his ex to ‘be kissing men in public.

Sources told Daily Mail at the time that Joe “would be happy to find love again and do all of that, but that is not even close to what is on the top of his mind as he wants to get on with the divorce and get on with his life.”
