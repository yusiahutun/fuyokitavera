Who Is Jordan Fisher on Dancing With the Stars? Plus More Questions From Week Four Answered!

It’s already week four of Dancing With the Stars — and the competition is heating up! After watching their favorite celebrity/pro dancer pairs in the ballroom tonight, fans have many burning questions about the hit ABC show. Scroll down to get them all answered!

Who is Jordan Fisher on Dancing With the Stars?

Fans were captivated by Jordan’s tribute to his adoptive parents on tonight’s episode, leaving many to wonder who he is. Turns out, the 23-year-old is an American singer, dancer, and actor, and is known for his roles in Liv and Maddie and Grease Live!, to name a few.

Is Artem Chigvintsev from Dancing With the Stars dating?

Artem was married from 2004 until 2005 to his partner Giselle Peacock. He was dating Torrey DeVitto from 2016 until their recent split in May. To the surprise of many fans, he even dated judge Carrie Ann Inaba from 2006 until 2008!

How old is Witney Carson?

The pro dancer is only 23 years old! She married her husband and high school sweetheart, Carson McAllister, in January 2016. So cute!

Who is Sharna Burgess’ partner on Dancing With the Stars?

Sharna is partnered with NBA star Derek Fisher this season, who most recently served as the head coach of the New York Knicks. Last season she was paired with Bonner Bolton and the two came in fifth place. The season before that, she and her partner James Hinchcliffe were runner-ups.

Is Derek Fisher married?

Not anymore. The NBA star was hitched to Candace Fisher from 2005 until 2016. She is also the mother of his daughter Tatum, who he tributed his DWTS dance tonight to after telling her cancer battle story.

What is Maksim Chmerkovskiy’s net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Maks is worth an estimated $3 million. His wife, Peta Murgatroyd, is worth a reported $400,000. Talk about a power couple!

Is Drew Scott gay?

He is not, but he’s happy to joke about the rumors! In a recent exclusive interview with Closer Weekly, he opened up about the false talk. “Good looking guys with good looking designs, they have to be gay!” Drew laughed.
