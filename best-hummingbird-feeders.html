10 Best Hummingbird Feeders 2024

The First Nature 16 oz. feeder is, hands down, our top pick for the best hummingbird feeder. It's a high-quality, incredibly well-reviewed product with an eye-catching red base, 10 feeding ports, and a perch that allows for easy viewing. It also features an anti-leak sealing ring, and reviewers say it's easy to clean, too.

One of our favorite features of this hummingbird feeder is the wide-mouth bottle and two-part base. Disassembly of this feeder takes no time, meaning it's incredibly quick and simple to clean. It also comes with a hook for easy hanging. The only negative with this feeder is that it's not the most attractive option. But for under $5, we'll take it.
