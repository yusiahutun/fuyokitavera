Jesse Eisenberg leaves London theatre with Alfie Allen following The Spoils performance

He was recently in Cannes promoting his latest film, Cafe Society.

But jet-setting Jesse Eisenberg didn't have time to stop and relax as he was soon whisked back to London to perform on the West End stage. 

So it's perhaps no surprise that the talented 32-year-old looked a little tired as he exited the theatre on Monday night.

Hectic schedule: Jesse Eisenberg, 32, looked a little tired as he exited the theatre on Monday night after performing in his play, The Spoils

Jesse is currently starring in dark comedy, The Spoils, alongside fellow actors, Alfie Allen and The Big Bang Theory star, Kunal Nayyar.

The play has been somewhat of a pet project for the Zombieland star, as he not only performs in it, but also wrote the script himself. 

And Jesse looked ever the creative as he headed home post-show, as he cut a casual figure in a round-neck grey jumper and charcoal snood. 

Wrapping up: Jesse looked ever the creative as he headed home post-show, as he cut a casual figure in a round-neck grey jumper and charcoal snood

Spot the difference? Jesse (pictured in 2011) is often seen looking dapper on the red capet and sporting a much fuller head of hair and

He also rounded off his laid-back style with a number of navy accessories, including a hooded jacket that kept him cosy in the spring air, with a thick fleecy lining, and a casual beanie hat. 

Despite a long day at work, Jesse still kept active as he appeared to get ready to cycle home and adjusted his lemon and silver helmet in preparation. 

And he made sure he was prepared for all eventualities in a pair of navy, New Balance trainers. 

Feeling blue: Alfie Allen cut an effortlessly off-duty figure in a sky-blue v neck t-shirt that he teamed with some loose fitting indigo jeans

Hard night's work: Alfie appeared to have worked up a sweat during his performance, as he sported ruffled hair and opted to tie his grey jumper around his waist

Alfie Allen also chose to follow in his co-star's footsteps with matching shoes. 

The actor, seemed far removed from his Game Of Thrones character, as he cut an effortlessly off-duty figure in a sky-blue v neck t-shirt that he teamed with some loose fitting indigo jeans. 

And he appeared to have worked up a sweat during his performance, as he sported ruffled hair and opted to tie his grey jumper around his waist. 

Sophisticated: Big Bang Theory star, Kunal Nayyar, looked typically dapper in some navy chinos and an on-trend denim shirt

Meanwhile, Kunal, looked typically dapper in some navy chinos and an on-trend denim shirt, which he covered with a brown jumper and a heavy black overcoat.

The 35-year-old also toted a slick tan briefcase, which he made sure to keep hold of even while posing for photos with fans. 

Despite their fame, all the men took the time to greet audience members who had waited patiently by the stage doors in the hope of taking a selfie with the stars. 

The Spoils is Eisenberg's third play and focuses on themes such as American privilege, family and relationships.
