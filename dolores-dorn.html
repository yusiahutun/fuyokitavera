Dolores Dorn (March 3, 1934 – October 5, 2019; born Dolores Heft) was an American film and television actress, co-star of The Bounty Hunter (1954), Uncle Vanya (1957), Underworld U.S.A. (1961), and several other films through 1985. On Broadway she was billed as Dolores Dorn-Heft. She died on October 5, 2019.
