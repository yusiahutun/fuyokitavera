Pervert, 28, makes sick child abuse video of his teenage stepdaughter

A pervert who secretly filmed his teenage stepdaughter's private parts is now behind bars awaiting sentencing after his partner found the footage on his phone.  

The Northern Rivers man, 28, appeared in Tweed Heads Local Court this week via video link from the Grafton Correctional Centre following the incident in December.

The mother-of-four, who had been dating the man for eight years, was checking the man's phone when she discovered the disturbing footage. 

A woman was horrified after discovering a video of her daughter filmed without her consent on her partner's phone (stock image)

She went straight to police after viewing footage showing her daughter, who is under the age of 16, the Northern Star reported.

The court heard the girl and another child were left in the 28-year-old's care on December 21.

The man is then alleged to have filmed the girl's private parts without her knowledge.   

According to court documents, the pair had been in a relationship for just under a decade and the woman also had her four children living at the house. 

Following a police investigation, the man was charged and has since pleaded guilty to producing child abuse material of his step-daughter.

Andrew Horowitz, the DPP prosecutor, said police facts suggested there was material of a 'similar nature' found on the man's smartphone.

The matter was adjourned for sentencing on March 20.
