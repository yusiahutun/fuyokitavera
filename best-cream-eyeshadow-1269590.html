    How to Choose the Best Cream Eyeshadow?

    Here are the parameters to consider before picking the best cream eyeshadow - 

    a) Formula- The formula you should pick depends a lot on your concerns. If you have sensitive skin, a dermatologically- tested or hypoallergenic formula would work best for you. On the other hand, if you are a mindful beauty lover, a vegan-friendly and cruelty-free formula is the right one!

    b) Blendability- The cream formula you are choosing must offer high blendability in order to ensure the natural finish that you need out of your makeup product.

    c) Finish- The finish you like depends on your personal preference. Creamy eyeshadows come in a variety of finishes, including matte, shimmer, glitter, and metallic finishes. For regular use, a matte one would work best. On the other hand, for subtle glam, a shimmer eyeshadow is what you need. Meanwhile, glitter eyeshadows are best for parties.

    Now that you have checked how you can choose the best cream eyeshadows, take a look at how you can apply them to your eye makeup effortlessly—

    How to Apply Cream Eyeshadows?

    Here's how you can do it—

    Step 1: First things first, if you want a vibrant and long-lasting outcome out of your eyeshadows, let's not forget the priming part. You can either apply an eyeshadow primer or a concealer.

    Step 2: Apply some loose powder with a fluffy brush to set the concealer, in case you are opting for it as a base.

    Step 3: Depending on the color palette you are opting for, you can take an eyeshadow brush, and apply the cream eyeshadow from the same color palette in the crease. Colors with matte finish like brown, taupe, mauve, pink, coral, and nude go excellent as a transition or a crease shade.

    Step 4: If you have shimmer or glitter cream eyeshadows, it is an excellent way to make your eyes vibrant with them by applying them all over the lids.

    Step 5: Lastly, you can anytime set the cream eyeshadows with a powder shadow of the same color with the same finish to get the saturated effect out of your eye makeup.

    Step 6: Apply coats of mascara or liner of your choice at last, to seal the deal.

    FAQ's

    Are cream eyeshadows suitable for all skin types?

    Yes, cream eyeshadows are excellent for all skin types. However, if you have particularly oily eyelids, this might not be a very good option.

    Can cream eyeshadows be used as a base for other eyeshadow products?

    Yes, cream eyeshadows can be used as a base for your eye makeup before you apply the powder eyeshadows to make the look more vibrant.

    Do cream eyeshadows crease, and how can I prevent it?

    Yes, since it is a creamy product applied to our eyelids that already have some natural creases, the product may crease in some cases. To prevent that, you can lightly press some loose powder over it once you witness the same.

    How long do cream eyeshadows typically last throughout the day?

    Depending on the formula, it can last for a minimum of 6 hours.

    Are cream eyeshadows difficult to blend?

    No, cream eyeshadows are some of the easiest products to start your eye makeup journey with because of their ease of blendability.

    Can I use cream eyeshadows for both everyday and dramatic makeup looks?

    Yes, of course, you can use cream eyeshadows for everyday eye makeup looks as well as a base for your dramatic party makeup looks. You can also deposit powder eyeshadows over cream ones to make the eye look more vibrant.

    Are cream eyeshadows suitable for mature skin?

    Cream eyeshadows may not look as good as it does on younger skin because of its tendency to get deposited on creases.

    Do cream eyeshadows have a tendency to smudge or transfer?

    Yes, cream eyeshadows have a tendency to smudge or transfer. However, if you invest in a transfer-proof or smudge-resistant formula, it may work wonders on your skin.

    Are cream eyeshadows safe for sensitive eyes?

    Yes, cream eyeshadows are very safe for sensitive eyes if you opt for a hypoallergenic or dermatologically-tested formula that won't cause any allergic reaction.

    How do I remove cream eyeshadow easily?

    You can take a makeup-removing wipe or a cotton pad with micellar water to remove your cream eyeshadow.

    Can I layer different shades of cream eyeshadow for a customized look?

    Yes, definitely, just like powder eyeshadows, you can play with different colors of cream eyeshadows to get the desired look.
