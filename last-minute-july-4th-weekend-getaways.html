11 Best 4th of July Getaways

July 4th doesn’t get much more iconic than at the exclusive and storied Ocean House, set in the charming enclave of Watch Hill, Rhode Island. Perched high above a bluff overlooking the Block Island Sound, this unrivaled oceanfront gem is, point blank, one of the best of the best–with a stunning private beach reserved for the property’s guests. The perfect place for a stroll along the water, a gourmet meal or a quick game of croquet on lush lawns overlooking the property, a stay at this beautiful Relais & Chateaux property is as exclusive as it gets.

A private escape from the crowds but near enough to the buzz of Watch Hill and Newport, if you want to venture out, enjoy tasty local flavors at Olympia Tea Room and try the delicious and briny Watch Hill oysters. The town of Watch Hill shines as a preppy patriotic enclave, and the Ocean House has its very own 4th of July fireworks that dazzle in the night sky. While there, taking part in their annual Independence Day Beach Ball is a must, and a spirited way to ring in the holiday. This year, the property has even unveiled a secret Veuve Clicquot Champagne Garden that is charming and private with stunning views.

Where to Stay: Ocean House, from $1,152 per night BOOK
