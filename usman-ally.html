Early Life and Childhood

Usman Ally was born on August 27, 1982, in the city of Eswatini which was formerly known as Swaziland. His age is 41 years as of 2023 and his full name is also Usman Ally.

Similarly, he is Pakistani and American by nationality and Muslim by religion. Moreover, his zodiac sign is Virgo which belongs to the element of Earth, according to astrologers.

Education

In terms of his educational background, he has done his schooling at the International School of Tanganyika and graduated in 2000.

Later, he attended Lewis & Clark College in Portland, Oregon, and majored in theater and cultural anthropology. After graduating there, he attended the University of Florida and graduated with a Master of Fine Arts in acting.

Career and Professional Life

Moving on to his career, he made his acting debut as Sunil in the short film titled The Group in 2008. Moreover, he has appeared in several stage productions such as The Elaborate Entrance of Chad Deity, The Jungle Book, and Around the World in 80 Days.

In 2012, he was featured as Ousman in the film titled Just Like a Woman. In 2013, he was featured as U.S.S. Vengeance Officer in the science fiction film Star Trek Into Darkness.

Currently, he resides in Los Angeles, California, and also, in video games he has lent his voice to lead villains in games such as Uncharted: The Lost Legacy as Asav, and in the Avengers game as MODOK, and many others.

He rose to fame quickly as his acting was loved by many of his fans. Due to his great acting, he won an Obie Award for his role in the famous play titled The Invisible Hand in 2015.
