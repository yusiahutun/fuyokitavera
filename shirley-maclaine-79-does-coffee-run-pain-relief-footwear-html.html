Shirley MacLaine, 79, does the coffee run in pain relief footwear

You’d be forgiven for thinking sprightly Shirley MacLaine had a spring in her step on Friday.

Not for the first time the 79-year-old actress caught the eye when she ventured out for an iced coffee from a local Starbucks in Malibu.

However, it wasn’t her large blue sun-hat that demanded attention, nor was it the colourful patchwork trousers that flapped about her ankles she strolled towards her car.

Shock absorbing: Shirley MacLaine steps out for an iced drink in a pair of Z-coiL pain-relief shoes on Friday

Added boost: Shirley's shoes are designed for people who suffer from mobility issues, including back and foot pain

No, what really caught the eye was her interesting choice of footwear.

Indeed, the casual white shoes had a large spring attached to the heel that, while appearing a little eccentric upon first glance, served a constructive purpose for the elderly star.

Indeed, Shirley was wearing a pair of Z-CoiL Pain-Relief footwear – a brand of shoe designed specifically for people with pain in their feet, joints, legs and lower back.

Out and about: Sprightly actress Shirley MacLaine enjoys a day out in sunny Malibu

According to the American brand’s website they aim to alleviate pain for people suffering from a range of conditions – amongst them ‘flat-feet’ and bunions – while reducing ‘impact-induced injuries’ caused by walking on hard surfaces. 

The actress’s shoes include a shock absorber containing a steel coil that apparently reduces pain caused by movement, while the extra bounce is said to provide the wearer with an added boost of energy.

Shirley had previously turned heads when she stepped out for some groceries sporting a large white sun-visor bearing the word ‘Malibu.’

Parental ad-visor-y: The actress wears a hard to miss sun-visor during a previous day out in Malibu

Time to go: Shirley loads up her car with groceries during a day out - minus the shock absorber shoes

On this occasion the Hollywood legend – who celebrates her 80th birthday next year – decided against her pain relief footwear, opting instead for a pair of conventional slip on shoes.

The older sister of actor Warren Beatty, Shirley has been a star since the 1950’s – when she made her first big screen appearance in Alfred Hitchcock’s The Trouble with Harry.

Her recent screen credits include a role in ITV drama Downton Abbey as Martha Levinson.
