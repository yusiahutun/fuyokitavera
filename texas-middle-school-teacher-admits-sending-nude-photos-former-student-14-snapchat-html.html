Texas middle school teacher sendt nude photos to former student over Snapchat

A Texas middle school teacher was arrested after confessing that she send multiple nudes of herself to a 14-year-old bot. 

Kylie Rexann Modisette, a 28-year-old English teacher at Huntington Middle School in Huntington, was arrested on Thursday after admitting to a Texas Ranger that she sent nude photos over Snapchat to the boy.

Modisette, who said she sent her former student a series of sexts in December, was charged with distribution of harmful material to a minor.

Kylie Rexann Modisette, 28, admitted to Texas Rangers in Huntington that she sent nude photos to herself to a 14-year-old boy over Snapchat. Modisette was the boy's English teacher when he attended Huntington Middle School

The boy, who is now in high school, deleted the photos from his phone after his friends took screenshots and distributed them throughout the school, according to KJAS.  

Huntington Independent School District superintendent David Flowers told the New York Daily News that Modiesette has been put on administrative leave while the district further investigates.
