Bio: Age, Birthday, Height

Onscreen Parents And Family Details

Millicent Simmonds has developed a special affinity with both her real and on-screen families. Her social media posts are evident of this fact. As she is often seen spending quality times with her real family, she has equally maintained close ties with her on-screen family of A Quiet Place.

In the movie, John Krasinski played the role of Millicent's father and Emily Blunt played her mother. But, Millicent's real-life mother is Emily Simmonds and she has a total of four siblings in her family.

Quick Facts About Her

  • When Millicent Simmonds was 12 months old, she lost her hearing ability and became deaf.  So, her family learned American Sign Language, according to her mother's decision. Millicent's hearing impairment didn’t stop her from pursuing a career in acting. When she was in school, she joined the drama club at Jean Massieu School for the deaf and participated in the Utah Shakespeare Festival.  
  • She got her first career breakthrough as an actress in a movie which was an adaptation of the novel, Wonderstruck. There, she had to do the role of Rose portraying the character of a deaf girl.  Her drama teacher recommended her for this role to director Todd Haynes.
    Her first movie Wonderstruck received huge praise and attention from the critics as it was also screened at the New York Film Festival. 
  • AP named Millicent one of the breakthrough entertainers of 2017. Similarly, Time magazine listed her role of Rose as one of the top 10 movie performances of 2017.
  • She also got nominated for a Critic’s Choice Award for Best Young Performer in 2018.
  • She is known for her mesmerizing role in the Sci-Fi/ horror movie A Quiet Place- which gave her more fame and limelight. The movie fared critically and commercially well. From the movie, she again got nominated in Critics Choice for Best Young Performer.
  • In 2019, she made two guest appearances in TV shows Andi Mack and This Close.
  • Millicent loves to spend her time with family and friends, and she is fond of reading, drawing, watching anime, and riding her motorcycle.
  • She has developed close ties with her A Quiet Place co-star, Noah Jupe.
