Misty Copeland Ties the Knot With Her Boyfriend of 10 Years

Misty Copeland wed her longtime boyfriend Olu Evans on Sunday, July 31, after 10 years together. The couple's big day took place at the Monage Hotel in Laguna Beach, California, where 100 friends and family came together to help celebrate the union.

Copeland's wedding plans were in motion shortly after the her engagement to Evans, an attorney, in August 2015. Unlike her glamorous onstage life as a ballerina, she preferred to have a more low-key wedding day, she told E! .

"I feel like I get to become a fairy princess on stage and wear incredible costumes and gowns to galas. So, for me, I just want to have a simple, as easy-going day as I can have and just not have it be a big show. I just want it to be simple and classy and not feel like I'm in a tutu," she said.

Copeland's vision became a reality with her intimate ceremony on Sunday, which reportedly took place overlooking the ocean with pink and white hydrangeas as decor.
