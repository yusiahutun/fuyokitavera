MAC Retro Matte Metallics - New MAC Liquid Lipstick Metallic

MAC Cosmetics just quietly dropped six new metallic shades of their massively popular Retro Matte lipsticks—but there's a catch. You can't buy them in stores, only online. The Retro Matte Metallic lipstick collection is shoppable right now on the brand's website. The six warm shades are perfect for fall—see them all and shop them below.

Our two favorites are Crowned, a blackened purple, and Foiled, a rose bronze. The formula dries down to a plush matte finish that still has a little sheen to it, thanks to the metallic shimmer.
