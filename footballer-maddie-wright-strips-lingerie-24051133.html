Footballer Maddie Wright strips to lingerie for sizzling clip teasing OnlyFans content

Ex-footballer Maddie Wright has teased her Twitter followers with a lingerie video to persuade them to join up to her new OnlyFans page.

The former Charlton Athletic and Millwall star shared a video of her tensing her sporty abs while wearing some black strappy lingerie to her 55,000 followers on May 5.

The tweet was captioned "Come chat to me on my OnlyFans tonight" with a tongue emoji followed by a link to her account on the subscription site.

Over 290 fans liked the tweet with blokes replying to call her "sexy" and tell her that she's "looking good as always".
