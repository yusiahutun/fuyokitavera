Jennifer Lopez Models the Cutest Sunglasses in Coach's Spring 2021 Sunwear Campaign

We've considered ourselves Jennifer Lopez fans for years decades, so we're well aware there are a few key pieces the actress, singer, designer, and businesswoman has kept in her closet forever. She loves some statement heels, she's rarely spotted without a bedazzled cup (yes, it's a full-on accessory!), and some of her best outfits include a pair of large, glamorous sunglasses.

That's why it makes a lot of sense that Lopez's latest batch of ads for Coach, the brand for which she's an ambassador, show the star modeling some sweet shades. Even though the rest of her look is toned-down, with Lopez wearing a white tee and a classic trench, the pink and gold addition instantly makes the outfit signature J.Lo.

We're now convinced that Coach's Sculpted Signature Hexagon Sunglasses, which are part of its spring 2021 sunwear collection, are the easiest way to nail Lopez's style. And, even better, purchasing a pair for ourselves won't leave a ridiculously large dent in our bank account. The classic, geometric design — which, not to mention, is both universally flattering and statement-making — retails for $185, and you can also have your pick from multiple colorways should you prefer black or brown over pink.

So, if you've ever wondered how to look as good in sunglasses as Lopez does, now's your chance. Head over to coach.com to purchase this exact style for yourself.
