Love Island dumping 'confirmed' as ITV star leaks fire pit detail never seen on camera

Love Island: All Stars could be having an imminent recoupling after an ITV star dropped a huge clue live on-air.

The Islanders have been living it up in the South African villa for the last three weeks, with just six contestants being dumped from the Island in that time. But now things could be all set to change after Love Island bosses dropped a hint about tonight's episode that meant fans would not want to miss out on the show.

Andi Peters was presenting the competition segments during Good Morning Britain on Monday (February 5) when he made the announcement. Coming to viewers live from South Africa, Peters was able to explore the outside area of the villa as the Islanders continued to sleep.

As Andi began his segments, viewers could see that the temperature in South Africa was a gentle 22 degrees Celcius. With that being just the morning temperature, Andi admitted that the contestants would be absolutely boiling as they basked in the summer sun.

But Andi was quick to show off a cool new piece of information about the firepit that seemed to give away the news of a huge recoupling on the cards. As Peters moved towards the firepit, he admitted that they had added an extra component to the iconic pit.

He then went on to reveal that around the firepit - where some of the most iconic moments of the show have taken place - ITV bosses have decided to put in air conditioning on to help cool Islanders down in the summer sun.

"Because it gets so hot here in South Africa, they have installed air conditioning around the fire pit," Andi explained. "To keep the Islanders cool when they are about to couple up."

He then gave another huge clue as he showed off the front of the villa, and dropped a bombshell by saying that the iconic metal love heart that guards the door of the villa will be seen on sets once again. As a result, Andi's words implied that there may be a new Islander about to set foot in the villa in tonight's episode - or, indeed, another dumping.

As well as the huge secrets Andi was spilling, Andi gave some extra titbits about the villa and how the show comes together. The villa has been completely re-decked for the new series, include a new Hideaway area and the inclusion of a luscious terrace and fancy bathrooms upstairs.

The news of a potential recoupling comes after an explosive night following the events of Movie Night. In tense scenes that aired on Sunday (February 4), Georgia Steel was seen to be two-timing her former partner Callum Jones by sweet talking new boy Tom Clare.

Love Island: All Stars airs every Sunday to Friday at 9pm on ITV2 and ITVX
