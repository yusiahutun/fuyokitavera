Thomas Brodie-Sangster and Talulah Riley are engaged! Elon Musk's two-time ex-wife CONFIRMS marriage

She was married to the world's richest man not just once but twice.

Now, Talulah Riley is to wed again, this time to baby-faced actor Thomas Brodie-Sangster.

The pair, who have been dating for two years, announced they had got engaged last night, ironically using Musk's Twitter platform to share their news.

Writing alongside a photograph of herself and Thomas, Talulah, 37, said: 'Very happy to share that after two years of dating, Thomas Brodie Sangster and I are engaged.'

The happy couple, who live in a grade two listed Manor House in the countryside, enjoyed a night out in London on Wednesday where the actress was seen with a diamond ring on her wedding finger.

They were seen leaving the celebrity haunt Chiltern Firehouse arm in arm along with their cocker spaniel.
