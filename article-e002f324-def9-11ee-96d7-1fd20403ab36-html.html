Erma Jo Gatlin | Obituaries

Mrs. Erma Jo Gatlin, 91, of Heidelberg passed away on March 9, 2024 at Comfort Care Nursing Home in Laurel. 

She was preceded in death by her husband Harold Gatlin; daughter Peggy Gardner; and parents Hill Hancock and Mae Bell Buckley Hancock. 

She is survived by her daughters Dianna Herring (Skipper) of Petal and Kim Morgan (Tony) of Brandon; son-in-law Jeff Gardner of Laurel; four grandchildren, Reagan Walters, Taylor Baughman, Chance Herring and Tyler Graham; and five great-grandchildren. 

Special thanks to Elna McGrew for always being there for her. 

The service will be at Memory Chapel Funeral Home on Monday, March 11 at 2 p.m. Visitation will begin at 1 p.m. Rev. Bill Boulton will officiate. Interment will be in Spring Hill Cemetery. 

Pallbearers will be Skipper Herring, Tony Morgan, Chance Herring, Tyler Graham, Trent Walters and Zachary Russell. 

To sign the online guestbook, visit; www.memorychapellaurel.com.
