What to look for in the most comfortable shoes

Edward Berthelot//Getty Images

When you’re shopping for comfortable sneakers, the most important thing is that they fit well. Your toes should have room to wiggle and should not touch the end of the shoe. Styles with foam cushioning will be soft underfoot, while rubber will be a bit more durable, but ultimately not as cushy.
