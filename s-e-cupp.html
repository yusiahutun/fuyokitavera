S.E. Cupp Biography

Firstly, Elizabeth started her career working for an online magazine and a public relations company. Later, she was hired by The New York Times where she writes and edits for the index Department. She also wrote articles for The Washington Post, New York Daily News, The American Spectator, Townhall, Newsmax, The Daily Caller and many other. 

Sarah also worked as a commentator for Mercury Radio Arts, featured her own show, SE Cupp on the insider Extreme broadcast. She also co-hosted TV show, The Cycle on MSNBC with Krystal Ball; political strategist. She was also a panelist on the CNN political debate show, Crossfire also on Real News From The Blaze featured on TheBlaze. Moreover, she co-author Why You're Wrong About the Right and authored Losing Our Religion: The Liberal Media's Attack on Christianity.  

Moving towards her personal life, Sarah Elizabeth married to John Goodwin, former chief of staff to Raul Labrador. They dated for several years and got engaged with each other. After a year an engagement they got married and gave birth to a son, John Davies Goodwin III. 

Sarah has earned wide recognition and wealth in her career as a journalist. Her current net worth is estimated to be around $ 1 Million.
