14 keepsake Queen Elizabeth II mugs &amp; tea cups to cherish forever

As the nation paid their respects to Queen Elizabeth II, who passed away at the age of 96 on September 8 last year, we’re still reflecting on her incredible reign as the UK’s longest-ever serving monarch.

Celebrating 70 years on the throne earlier in 2022 with the Platinum Jubilee, Queen Elizabeth II held a special place in people’s hearts not just as their monarch, but as a woman, a mother, a grandmother, a great-grandmother and a devoted wife.

Reflecting the fondness of the nation for Queen Elizabeth II, searches for royal memorabilia were at an all-time high following the state funeral, with sales predicted to continue throughout this year too.
